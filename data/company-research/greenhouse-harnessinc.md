Provider/key: greenhouse:harnessinc | company_type: product

What they do: Harness is a software delivery platform that combines AI-native CI/CD, developer self-service, infrastructure provisioning, observability, security testing (AppSec/Traceable), cost optimization, and feature flags into a unified DevOps platform. Their mission is to enable 30 million developers to "deliver code to their users quickly, reliably and efficiently." The platform separates CI from CD into purpose-built modules sharing common infrastructure.
