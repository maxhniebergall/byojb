# Guidewire — research
Provider/key: workday:guidewire/external | company_type: product

What they do: Guidewire is a B2B SaaS company that builds cloud-based insurance management software for property and casualty (P&C) insurers worldwide. Their platform combines core insurance operations (policy, billing, claims), digital tools, analytics, and AI capabilities. They are the dominant vendor in the P&C insurance software market.

How they describe themselves: "Transform the way the world's P&C insurers do business." Core cultural values: determination, collaboration, continuous improvement, and bravery. Describes team as "uniquely connected, with each of us bringing a little quirkiness — and a little genius — to the table." Flexible approach to work while maintaining physical offices in 17 countries.

Size / stage / funding: Public company (NYSE: GWRE). ~3,000+ employees. Enterprise-grade, mature company. Founded 2001, San Mateo CA HQ. Revenue ~$1B+ ARR. Serves 540+ P&C insurers in 40+ countries.

Locations / HQ: San Mateo, CA (HQ). Offices in 17 countries including US, Canada (Toronto), UK, Ireland, India (Bangalore), Poland, Japan, Australia. Toronto office active with multiple engineering roles.

Remote policy: "Flexible approach to work." Some roles are remote, some hybrid, some onsite. Remote roles appear to exist but are location-specific. Senior Backend Engineer - DevOps roles are posted as "Poland — Remote," suggesting remotes are geographically scoped. Engineering roles span India, Poland, Toronto, and US primarily.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Partial / mixed. Guidewire has a Toronto Canada office and actively posts there (19 Toronto openings on ZipRecruiter, 158 on LinkedIn Canada). Remote roles appear to be scoped to specific countries (e.g., DevOps posted Poland-only). A "Staff Full Stack Engineer (Cloud Applications R&D) — Remote/Toronto" role confirms Canada remote exists for some positions. Not universally remote-Canada eligible for all roles in the batch.

Engineering & tech: Cloud platform engineering (Kubernetes, AWS, CI/CD, TeamCity). Platform roles involve microservices, cloud-native architecture, DevOps/SRE practices. Languages include Java, Python, some Go. Observability stack includes Datadog, OpenTelemetry, ELK. AI capabilities being built into the insurance platform. Product Development & Operations (PDO) org is the main engineering org.

Notable / other: Dominant P&C insurance SaaS vendor. Well-established company, not startup. Engineering is focused on enterprise insurance cloud platform — not cutting-edge infrastructure. Consulting ecosystem exists separately (SAPSOL, TMNAS) but Guidewire itself is a product company. Stable, mature business.

Open relevant roles (sample): Senior Backend Engineer - DevOps, Software Engineer II (Java backend), Senior Software Engineer - AI, Senior Software Engineer - Application Platform, Software Engineer III - AI Services, Senior Software Engineer (Platform Engineering), Mid Software Engineer - Python, Software Engineer II (Datadog/OpenTelemetry/ELK)
