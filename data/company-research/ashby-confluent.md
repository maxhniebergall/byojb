# Confluent — research
Provider/key: ashby:confluent | company_type: product
What they do: Confluent provides a cloud-native data streaming platform built on Apache Kafka and Apache Flink. They enable organizations to connect, process, and store real-time data streams for intelligence, analytics, and applications.
How they describe themselves: Guided by the mission to "set data in motion." They define their culture through five core values: "Earn Our Customers' Love," "Smart, Humble, and Empathetic," "Be Fired Up and Get Stuff Done," "Tasteful, Not Wasteful," and "One Team." Their tone is highly technical, developer-centric, and focused on customer outcomes.
Size / stage / funding: Public company (NASDAQ: CFLT), established, ~2,800+ employees.
Locations / HQ: Mountain View, California (HQ) with global hubs.
Remote policy: Remote-first, offering high flexibility to work from home or in-office.
Remote-Canada eligibility: Verified yes. They actively hire remote workers across Canada, specifically listing remote roles in British Columbia and Ontario.
Engineering & tech: Focuses on distributed systems engineering, JVM performance, data storage, and network transport. Heavy use of Java, Scala, Go, C++, Kubernetes, AWS, GCP, and Azure.
Notable / other: Prominent leader in data infrastructure; maintains confluent.io/blog and developer.confluent.io. High talent bar with high ownership, but generally well-regarded engineering practices.
Open relevant roles (sample): Senior Software Engineer, Connect Platform; Staff Software Engineer, Kora Compute; Senior Distributed Systems Engineer.
