# Unity — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:unity3d | company_type: product
What they do: Unity Technologies develops the Unity game engine, a leading real-time 3D development platform. They provide tools, platforms, and services (including ads and analytics monetization) to create, run, and monetize interactive 3D, VR, and AR content.
How they describe themselves: Grounded in the core belief that "the world is a better place with more creators in it." Their cultural principles emphasize leading with empathy and respect, communicating with candor, acting with urgency, and prioritizing the greater good.
Size / stage / funding: Public company (NYSE: U), established enterprise, ~5,000+ employees.
Locations / HQ: San Francisco, California (HQ) with major offices in Montreal, Quebec, and Vancouver, British Columbia.
Remote policy: Hybrid or remote-first depending on the specific team/role.
Remote-Canada eligibility: Verified yes. They actively hire and support fully remote software engineering roles within Canada, specifically in British Columbia, Ontario, and Quebec.
Engineering & tech: High focus on real-time graphics rendering, compiler optimization, distributed systems (for multiplayer hosting), and ML inference systems (Unity Sentis). Tech stack is heavily C++, C# (for game logic), and Go/Python for cloud backend and ML operations.
Notable / other: High-scale graphics and multiplayer networking tech. They have experienced business restructurings recently, which is a watch-item for team stability.
Open relevant roles (sample): Senior Distributed Systems Engineer; Software Engineer, Multiplayer Services; Infrastructure Engineer, Ads Core.
