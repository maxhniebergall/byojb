# Zenara Health — research
Provider/key: workable:zenara-health | company_type: product

What they do: Zenara Health is a very early-stage AI-native healthcare SaaS startup building clinical software for mental health practices. Their product (Zenara Flow) is a psychiatric practice management platform with modules for patient assessment (Assess — live), outcomes monitoring (Monitor — coming 2026), and care coordination (Care — coming 2026). The founder (Ravi Hariprasad) is a practicing psychiatrist in San Francisco; the product is informed by 10+ years of psychiatric practice and experience with large-scale programs at Kaiser and Headspace Health. Secondary product is ZenOS (behavioral health platform for psychiatry practices). Also described as combining telemedicine, telepharma, and medical billing.

How they describe themselves: "AI-Native Healthcare SaaS" is their primary positioning. They describe the platform as encoding workflows from a live psychiatry practice and automating intake/administrative burden. "Dedicated to transforming access and quality of mental wellness services." Tone is clinical/technical, founder-led, early-stage.

Size / stage / funding: Very early stage (pre-product launch for core modules). Assess is available; Monitor and Care are "coming 2026." No disclosed funding or employee count. Hiring across DevOps, software engineering, Head of AI, Head of Engineering — suggesting very small founding team being built out. Free trial offered through July 2026.

Locations / HQ: San Francisco, CA (founder location, 415 area code). No physical office described.

Remote policy: All job postings are remote. However, roles are posted for India specifically — "4-8hrs daily overlap with US Pacific time" required, and explicitly listed as "Remote across India."

Remote-Canada eligibility: No — roles explicitly state "Remote across India" as the eligible location. Canada is not included. The DevOps & Security Engineer and Senior Software Engineer roles are India-based remote positions.

Engineering & tech: AI-native platform, DevOps/security (CI/CD pipelines, healthcare data compliance, HIPAA), cloud infrastructure, LLM/AI workflows. Very early-stage stack. Software engineering involves clinical AI and healthcare platform work.

Notable / other: This is a very small, founder-led, pre-revenue startup. Hiring is globally dispersed (India for engineering, US for leadership) suggesting a cost-arbitrage model for technical roles. The company may not yet have Series A funding. Described as "AI-hype adjacent" in that "AI-Native" is a primary marketing claim, but the underlying clinical use case is legitimate. Remote hiring for tech roles is India-only at this stage.

Open relevant roles (sample): DevOps & Security Engineer - AI-Native Healthcare SaaS; Senior Software Engineer (AI-Native) - Healthcare Platform
