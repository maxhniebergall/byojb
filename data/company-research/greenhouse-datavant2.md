What they do: Datavant builds secure, neutral data logistics infrastructure that allows healthcare organizations (payers, providers, life sciences, and government agencies) to protect, match, and share health data securely. Their platform aims to solve fragmentation across the healthcare ecosystem to improve patient outcomes.
How they describe themselves: Datavant operates on "The Datavant Way," defining their culture around high performance and autonomy. Their key traits are "Smart, Nice, and Get Things Done." They offer "More Responsibility, Fewer Rules" to foster ownership, push decision-making to the edges, and promote a growth mindset ("Growth Over Comfort"). They compare their team to a professional sports team rather than a family.
