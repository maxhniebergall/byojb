# Pinecone — research
Provider/key: ashby:pinecone | company_type: product

What they do: Pinecone is a managed vector database platform purpose-built for AI applications, particularly retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), semantic search, and recommendation systems. They offer a serverless vector database that stores and queries high-dimensional embeddings at scale. Their product is widely used in LLM-powered applications as the retrieval layer. Founded 2019.

How they describe themselves: Three core values: "Be a pro — Own your work, push boundaries, and strive for simplicity." "Be yourself — Be authentic, speak your truth, seek feedback, and know you are accepted." "Be a friend — Earn trust, extend help, listen intently, and commit to shared decisions." They describe their team as "world-class scientists and engineers who have built large scale ML applications" from AWS, Databricks, Google, Microsoft. Engineering areas: database internals, cloud infrastructure, search, information retrieval, AI. Mission: "The vector database to build knowledgeable AI."

Size / stage / funding: Raised $138M total. Investors: Andreessen Horowitz (led Series B), ICONIQ, Menlo Ventures, Wing Venture Capital. ~79-person engineering team described as fully remote/globally distributed (as of earlier data). NY and Tel Aviv offices. Not publicly traded. Exact current employee count and latest valuation not confirmed in recent sources.

Locations / HQ: New York City HQ (127 W 26th St). Tel Aviv office. Engineering team described as globally distributed. No Canadian office listed.

Remote policy: "Hybrid in-office/remote workforce." Offers WFH Equipment Stipend and Flexible PTO. Engineering team was described as "globally distributed" suggesting significant remote hiring. NY and Tel Aviv are the only confirmed office locations.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Unclear — globally distributed engineering team suggests possible Canadian remote, but no explicit Canada hiring confirmation found. No Canadian office. Verify per role listing.

Engineering & tech: Vector database internals, search and retrieval infrastructure, indexing algorithms (HNSW, IVF), distributed systems, cloud infrastructure (AWS, GCP), embedding pipelines, query optimization, ML platform integration. Recent product: Dedicated Read Nodes (Dec 2025) for predictable vector workloads. Significant scaling work in late 2025. Stack likely includes Go/Rust/C++ for database core, Python for client SDKs. Team veterans from AWS, Databricks, Splunk, Yahoo.

Notable / other: Pinecone is the dominant managed vector database by mindshare in the LLM/AI application ecosystem. However, the product is an infrastructure enabler for AI applications (not core system infrastructure). Competition from Weaviate, Qdrant, Milvus, pgvector. Raised capital in early AI boom; hasn't announced major funding rounds recently, suggesting either stability or quiet period. December 2025 scaling milestone (Dedicated Read Nodes) shows continued product investment.
