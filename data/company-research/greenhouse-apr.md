# aPriori — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:apr | company_type: product

What they do: aPriori (apr.io) is a crypto/blockchain infrastructure company — NOT the aPriori Technologies manufacturing software company. aPriori is an MEV (Maximal Extractable Value)-powered liquid staking protocol built natively for the Monad blockchain. Users stake $MON tokens and receive liquid $aprMON tokens in return, accruing yield from both validator rewards and MEV capture. The company describes itself as "the first MEV-powered staking protocol" and operates an "Intelligent Order Flow Coordination Layer For High-Performance Blockchains."

How they describe themselves: "Democratizing MEV for everyone." The company's engineering focuses on MEV infrastructure, block building, transaction ordering, and low-latency execution. Founded by veterans from Jump Crypto/Pyth, Coinbase, Citadel Securities, Amber, and Flow Traders. Tone is crypto-native, technically demanding.

Size / stage / funding: Raised $8M seed in July 2024 led by Pantera Capital at $100M valuation; follow-on $20M raise in August 2025, total $30M+ raised. Investors include Pantera Capital, Binance Labs, HashKey Capital, IMC Trading, Flow Traders. Small startup team.

Locations / HQ: Fully remote, no specific HQ. "We are a completely remote company so you can work from anywhere."

Remote policy: 100% remote, no geographic restrictions stated. "Flexible work culture." Note: explicit disclaimer "we cannot provide sponsorship if you wish to work in the US" — implying non-US-based hiring or self-employment arrangements.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Likely yes — fully remote with no geographic restrictions stated; Canada not explicitly mentioned but no country restrictions listed either. The US sponsorship disclaimer suggests non-US candidates (including Canadians) are a natural target.

Engineering & tech: Roles focus on MEV infrastructure, block building, relay communication, validator-client integration, block propagation. Tech stack: C++, Rust, Golang for low-latency systems. Also hiring generalist backend for liquid staking/DeFi APIs (Go, Rust, Python, Node.js, PostgreSQL). Extremely performance-critical, adversarial-network-conditions engineering.

Notable / other: Part of the Monad blockchain ecosystem. Integrations with DeFi platforms: Phantom, Pancakeswap, Curvance, token bridge via Stargate Finance. This is a crypto-native protocol, not a SaaS product company.
