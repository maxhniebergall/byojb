# Invoca — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:invoca | company_type: product

What they do: Invoca is a conversation intelligence and call tracking platform that helps marketers and revenue teams connect marketing spend to revenue outcomes. They provide AI-powered analytics on phone calls — tracking attribution, analyzing conversation content, and powering AI agents for contact center engagement. Use cases span marketing analytics, contact center AI, and customer engagement across industries including automotive, healthcare, telecom, and financial services.

How they describe themselves: "The Data + AI Platform to Drive Revenue Growth." They describe their culture as "inclusive and egoless" with values around "figure it out," continuous improvement, customer focus, and mutual support. The company is Series F and considers itself a market leader in call intelligence / conversation analytics.

Size / stage / funding: Series F company. Raised $202M total from Accel, Upfront Ventures, Morgan Stanley, and others. Valuation ~$1.1B (unicorn). ~389 employees as of 2026 (down from 422 in 2024 — some workforce contraction). Revenue $103.8M ARR in 2024 (up from $79.5M in 2023). Founded 2008 in Santa Barbara, CA.

Locations / HQ: Santa Barbara, California, USA. Remote-first for most roles. Some roles explicitly mention US and Canada eligibility.

Remote policy: Remote-friendly, US and Canada eligible for many engineering roles. Staff AI Platform Engineer position explicitly listed as Canada-eligible. Staff Applied AI Engineer salary range $180k-$273k available to US and Canada candidates. A Software Architect role was explicitly "remote, open to candidates located in the United States and Canada only."

Remote-Canada eligibility: Yes — confirmed for multiple engineering roles. Multiple job postings explicitly list both US and Canada as eligible locations.

Engineering & tech: Platform engineering (Ruby/Rails, CI/CD, authentication/authorization), AI/ML platform (RAG systems, LLM serving, agentic workflows), data & AI platform (BigQuery, Kafka, ML pipeline infrastructure). Staff AI Platform role involves building core APIs, SDKs, and reusable primitives for AI/agentic capabilities. Also has Senior ML Engineer role based in Nova Scotia, Canada. Stack: Ruby, Python, JavaScript, MySQL, Redis, Kafka, Terraform, Docker, Kubernetes.

Notable / other: $1B+ valuation unicorn in a mature SaaS vertical (call analytics/attribution). Slight headcount decline 2024-2026 suggests some efficiency pressure. Real-time telephony + AI is technically complex. Company has been operating for 16+ years with substantial recurring revenue.

Open relevant roles (sample): Sr. Software Engineer I (platform/CI-CD); Staff AI Platform Engineer (Remote, Canada eligible)
