# Travoom — research
Provider/key: smartrecruiters:travoom | company_type: product

What they do: Travoom is a Santa Monica / Austin-based startup that has pivoted from travel experiences (bucket list travel marketplace) to building OleOle — a football (soccer) focused social network, messaging platform, and sports super app. The platform targets football fans globally, combining social features, video (TikTok-style short-form), messaging (Twitter/X-style), and a blockchain-based token economy (OleX token on Solana). The "Travoom" brand itself is paused as the team focuses entirely on OleOle.

How they describe themselves: "The marketplace for bucket list travel experiences" (original mission, now paused). Current focus is OleOle: "a football-focused social network and messaging platform for the world's biggest sport." The company has 30+ years of collective experience in sports and travel. Currently in prototype/wireframe completion phase, building the native Rust backend.

Size / stage / funding: Pre-funding / bootstrapped. The company lost funding approximately 2 months before some job postings and is now operating on deferred compensation + sweat equity while securing funding. No confirmed VC round. Team members work under deferred compensation until funding closes. Very early stage — effectively a founding team looking for co-founders and senior engineers who will accept equity-only/deferred comp.

Locations / HQ: Austin, TX (500 W 2nd St listed on job postings). Santa Monica, CA also listed. Job postings listed in Austin, TX with no explicit remote work policy clarified. One posting noted "this role requires some travel."

Remote policy: Unclear. Jobs are listed under specific city addresses (Austin, TX). No confirmed remote work policy. The Austin and Santa Monica listings suggest location-dependent roles, though some job boards classify them as "remote." One Rust engineer posting explicitly listed as "United States Remote" on some aggregators but the SmartRecruiters primary posting lists Austin, TX.

Remote-Canada eligibility: No (likely disqualified on multiple grounds). Postings are US-location-based, company has no Canadian presence, and compensation is deferred (equity/sweat equity until funding closes). Even if remote, the deferred compensation model is a significant practical barrier.

Engineering & tech: Rust (required, async-first — Actix, Axum, Tokio), PostgreSQL, WebAssembly, gRPC, GraphQL (nice-to-have), Solana blockchain, real-time messaging infrastructure, video delivery pipelines, token-based incentive systems. Stack is native Rust throughout. Requires 4+ years production Rust experience and 7-12+ years overall backend experience. Very specialized Rust-centric stack.

Notable / other: Extremely high-risk profile: pre-funding, deferred compensation, seeking co-founders, paused primary product, building in Rust+blockchain simultaneously. Company lost prior funding. Multiple "possible co-founder role" postings suggest the company is still assembling its founding team. Rust+WebAssembly+Solana stack is technically interesting but the business risk is extreme. Not a viable employer under current circumstances for a stable IC role.
