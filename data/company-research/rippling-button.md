# Button — research
Provider/key: rippling:button | company_type: product

What they do: Button is an AI-powered mobile commerce optimization platform that routes user taps to the highest-converting destination (typically brand apps). Their platform serves retailers, publishers, advertisers, and creators by providing smart routing, privacy-safe attribution, and first-party data intelligence. They have driven over $12B in commerce and processed $25B in all-time sales on-platform.

How they describe themselves: "Shape the future of commerce" — focused on "great products, smart teams, and meaningful impact." They position the platform as building "journeys" rather than just links, using AI and deterministic first-party data to optimize mobile commerce outcomes. Claims 99.9% platform uptime, 300% conversion lift, and 5,000+ apps optimized.

Size / stage / funding: Founded 2014, NYC HQ. Raised $67.3M total over 7 rounds; latest was Series C in June 2025 (backed by PSG, Redpoint, Norwest, Icon Ventures). Surpassed $1B in monthly commerce driven in March 2024. Estimated ~50-100 employees (engineering-heavy startup).
