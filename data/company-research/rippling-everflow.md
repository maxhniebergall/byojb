# Everflow — research
Provider/key: rippling:everflow | company_type: product

What they do: Everflow is a bootstrapped SaaS partner marketing and performance tracking platform. It enables brands and agencies to track every performance marketing channel, attribute revenue, manage partnerships (affiliates, influencers, referrals, media buying), and process payments. Built API-first, it targets e-commerce, B2B, insurance, gaming, and telehealth verticals.

How they describe themselves: "Scale Affiliates & AI Agents With Confidence" — "full transparency" on partner performance and "attribution for every channel and user activity." Core values listed as Ownership, Mindfulness, Excellence, and Connection. Culture described as collaborative with emphasis on work-life balance, async work, and remote flexibility. Bootstrapped identity is central: "profitable bootstrapped company grown through happy customers and referrals."

Size / stage / funding: ~120-130 employees. Bootstrapped — no outside funding. ~$28-30M ARR (as of 2025). Revenue per employee ~$250K. Growing 25-30% annually. Founded May 2016 by Sam Darawish and co-founders.

Locations / HQ: San Francisco (Mountain View, CA), Montreal, Quebec (Canada), Amsterdam (Netherlands). Distributed across all timezones.

Remote policy: "We are distributed across all timezones, allowing our team flexibility around where they work and live." Asynchronous work culture. Engineering roles listed in the job board show Montreal, Canada-based postings. "Hybrid work & growth opportunities" advertised.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Yes — confirmed. Engineering roles (Senior Software Engineer, Software Engineer AI Platform) are posted with Montreal, Canada as the location. The company has a significant Canadian office in Montreal and hires engineers there.

Engineering & tech: Platform built API-first; integrates with 50+ advertising platforms. Engineering involves distributed systems, performance tracking at scale (1.3B events/year, 776M conversions, $3.8B revenue processed), real-time data pipelines. Current openings suggest Scala/backend focus (Senior Software Engineer, AI Platform Engineer). Small, tight-knit engineering team.

Notable / other: Founded by former Moolah Media executives. Profitable and bootstrapped — no VC pressure. 96% of employees would recommend working there. Offices in Montreal give Canadian hiring a strong foundation. The "Ingénieur logiciel senior" and "Ingénieur logiciel, plateforme d'IA" Montreal roles confirm French-language Canadian office.

Open relevant roles (sample): Senior Software Engineer (Montreal, Canada), Software Engineer AI Platform (Montreal, Canada), Senior Software Development Engineer in Test — Toronto, Canada
