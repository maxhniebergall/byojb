# Chainstack — research
Provider/key: bamboohr:chainstack | company_type: product

What they do: Chainstack is a managed blockchain infrastructure and RPC node service provider. They offer developers and enterprises access to 70+ blockchain networks (Ethereum, Solana, Arbitrum, and others) through managed nodes, global node infrastructure, and dedicated nodes. Their products include Global Node, Unlimited Node, and Managed Dedicated Nodes — essentially "AWS for blockchain nodes." They focus on making blockchain infrastructure accessible, reliable, and scalable for Web3 applications in DeFi, NFT, gaming, and analytics.

How they describe themselves: "Create the most accessible and easy to use platform to build, run and scale blockchain applications" and "help accelerate blockchain adoption by enterprises globally." They present themselves as an AI-first, flat-structure, no-bureaucracy company with cutting-edge tech. Co-founded in 2018 by Laurent Dedenis (20+ yrs managing tech companies) and Eugene Aseev (CTO, 10+ yrs R&D/cybersecurity). Culture is described as multinational, flexible schedules, high-ownership.

Size / stage / funding: Founded 2018, HQ Singapore. ~45–57 employees. Total funding ~$6M (Series A round May 2024, investors: Sygnum, Unicorn Factory Ventures, SBI Ven Capital, Ventech China, Azimut). Revenue ~$5M.

Locations / HQ: Singapore (HQ), offices/hubs also in Dubai, Yerevan, London, Belgrade. Fully distributed team globally.

Remote policy: Fully remote. All positions remote-first. Company runs remote hubs in multiple cities. Flexible schedules. Compensation in USD.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Unclear / role-dependent. The "Infrastructure Engineer (LATAM time zone)" role specifies Americas timezone — Mountain Time Canada overlaps. However the "Senior Software Engineer (Golang)" role specifies EU timezone (Western/Central/Eastern EU), which excludes Canada. Each role has its own timezone constraint. Canada is not explicitly excluded at the company level but individual roles may be timezone-gated.

Engineering & tech: Blockchain infrastructure engineering (Ethereum, Solana, etc.), Go (Golang), Python, distributed systems, Kubernetes, cloud providers (multi-cloud), CI/CD (GitLab), Ansible, Terraform, shell scripting. Engineering is organized around infrastructure, data indexing, and product teams. Flat hierarchy, high ownership culture.

Notable / other: Focused Web3 infrastructure play. Stock options offered. Operates in a niche but technically deep domain. Hubs for relocation available. Company is early-stage/growth (Series A, ~$5M revenue, ~50 people). Active in CNCF-adjacent ecosystem.

Open relevant roles (sample): Infrastructure Engineer (LATAM time zone); Senior Software Engineer (Golang)
