# Onyx Capital Group Limited — research
Provider/key: workable:onyx-capital-group | company_type: product

What they do: Onyx Capital Group is a technology-driven energy derivatives market maker and liquidity provider. They describe themselves as the world's #1 liquidity provider for energy derivatives, trading over $3.2 trillion in notional value annually. Products include Onyx Commodities (trading), the Flux Terminal (data/analytics platform), and Axis (trading division). The company is at the intersection of global energy markets, financial data, and proprietary engineering.

How they describe themselves: "The world's #1 liquidity provider for energy derivatives." Emphasizes "software craftsmanship as a genuine competitive advantage," TDD, observability-first design, and high-performance engineering. Culture is described as high-intensity with "lucrative benefits and fast career progression." Hiring is framed around "exceptional people" willing to work challenging hours. Registered in the UK (Companies House number: 14234006).

Size / stage / funding: Private, UK-registered company. Exact employee count and funding details not publicly disclosed. Positions themselves as a profitable trading operation rather than a VC-backed startup. Engineering hub being established in Sri Lanka.

Locations / HQ: London, UK (headquarters). Actively building an engineering hub in Sri Lanka. No other locations confirmed.

Remote policy: Remote roles are available — but the open platform/infra and software engineering roles are specifically scoped to Sri Lanka remote, providing coverage during Singapore and early Dubai/UK business hours. Not a globally distributed remote employer.

Remote-Canada eligibility: No. All engineering roles in the open postings are explicitly restricted to Sri Lanka. The Senior Platform Infrastructure Engineer and Senior Software Engineer roles both state "Sri Lanka only" or "Remote - Sri Lanka." No evidence of Canadian hiring for engineering roles.

Engineering & tech: Stack includes Microsoft Azure (compute, networking, managed services), Terraform (IaC), Kubernetes (production), Python (automation), GitHub/Azure DevOps/GitLab for CI/CD. Also uses Prometheus, Grafana, Azure Monitor. C# mentioned as nice-to-have. The platform powers live trading infrastructure for energy derivatives.

Notable / other: Onyx is a pure-play proprietary trading and market-making firm, not an enterprise software vendor. Engineering serves internal trading systems. The Sri Lanka engineering hub strategy is a cost/timezone arbitrage play. Separate entity from "Onyx Capital Management" (investment advisory) and "Onyx App" — these are different companies.

Open relevant roles (sample): Rust Software Engineer (Sri Lanka); Senior Platform Infrastructure Engineer (Sri Lanka); Senior Software Engineer (Sri Lanka)
