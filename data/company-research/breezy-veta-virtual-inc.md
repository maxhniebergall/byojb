# Veta Virtual — research
Provider/key: breezy:veta-virtual-inc | company_type: outsourcing

What they do: Veta Virtual Inc. (founded 2021, Toronto Canada) provides virtual receptionist and virtual assistant services to businesses across Canada and the US. They employ live human receptionists who handle inbound/outbound calls, appointment scheduling, call overflow, and customer experience for clients in legal, accounting, medical, construction, and marketing sectors. Core value proposition: professional outsourced reception at lower cost than in-house staff. They also run a "Remote Talent" division. Despite the DevOps job posting on their Breezy portal, the core business is a virtual receptionist/BPO company.

How they describe themselves: "We specialize in Customer Experience and Virtual Assistance services, helping businesses across Canada and the US thrive." They present as a "rapidly growing start-up" focused on personalized human service. Clutch-reviewed, 62 reviews. Tone is customer-service and culture focused.

Size / stage / funding: Founded 2021, Toronto. ~20 total employees (BuiltIn data). Startup/growth stage. No disclosed funding. Bootstrapped. HQ Toronto, Ontario but primarily hires from LATAM and distributed regions.

Locations / HQ: Toronto, Ontario, Canada (HQ). Remote-first. Staff primarily in Latin America (Argentina, Brazil, Colombia, Costa Rica, Mexico, Nicaragua) and worldwide.

Remote policy: Fully remote. Most positions are "fully remote, no location restrictions" or LATAM-specific. Company itself is based in Canada but hires globally.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Unclear / unlikely for DevOps role. The "Senior DevOps Engineer – Cloud Infrastructure" role is listed as both "Latin America Remote" and "Worldwide" in different listings. The Breezy portal lists it as available in multiple LATAM countries and worldwide. While the company is headquartered in Toronto, the DevOps role appears to target LATAM talent primarily. Canada eligibility for this specific role is not confirmed but cannot be ruled out given the "Worldwide" designation on one listing.

Engineering & tech: For the DevOps role: AWS (Terraform/IaC, EKS/Kubernetes, RDS, networking, compute, storage), CI/CD pipeline design and optimization. The engineering work appears to support an internal fintech/SaaS platform (one job listing describes "scalable cloud-native infrastructure for a global fintech platform"). This is notable — suggesting Veta Virtual may have a tech product in addition to their BPO services.

Notable / other: A virtual receptionist / outsourcing company hiring a DevOps engineer is unusual and suggests they may be building internal SaaS infrastructure. Very small company (20 employees). The "fintech platform" reference in one job posting is unexplained relative to their core BPO business. Could be a platform for their reception operations. Company is Canadian-founded but global-staffed. Low-charisma fit for a backend/infrastructure IC seeking stable, well-scoped engineering work.
