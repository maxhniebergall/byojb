# Reach Security — research
Provider/key: ashby:reach | company_type: product

What they do: Reach Security is an AI-native cybersecurity posture management platform. Their product identifies and remediates security control misconfigurations, detects configuration drift, and activates underutilized capabilities across existing security stacks. The platform integrates with major security vendors (CrowdStrike, Palo Alto Networks, Microsoft, SentinelOne, Proofpoint) and IT systems (ServiceNow, Jira). They describe themselves as bridging the gap between "knowing where you're exposed" and taking action to fix it — a CTEM (Continuous Threat Exposure Management) solution.

How they describe themselves: "Securing the future, together." Mission is to help organizations "move from awareness to action, at scale" through clarity, prioritization, and automation for security remediation. Values: innovation and collaboration, customer-first, transparency, execution at scale. Named Best CTEM Solution 2026, recognized by Gartner for domain-specific AI for SecOps.
