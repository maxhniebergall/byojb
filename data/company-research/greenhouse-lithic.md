# Lithic — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:lithic | company_type: product

What they do: Lithic is a card issuing and payment infrastructure platform. They provide developer-first APIs for fintech and banking companies to build, issue, and manage payment card programs (debit, credit, prepaid — physical and virtual). The platform handles card issuance, transaction processing (direct network connections to Visa, Mastercard, Amex), fraud controls, ledgering, ACH/wire transfers, and program management. Originated from scaling infrastructure at Privacy.com.

How they describe themselves: "A simple, developer-first API" for card programs. "We've supported our employees in working from wherever they need to be" since founding. Remote-distributed culture with heavy async communication ("We Slack a lot"). Serves "100+ high growth brands" processing "billions in volume." Backed by Bessemer Venture Partners, Index Ventures, Spark Capital.

Size / stage / funding: ~193 employees (as of April 2026). Total funding: $121M over 5 rounds. Latest known round: Series C (2022). Private company. Founded 2018. HQ in New York City (SoHo office).

Locations / HQ: HQ in New York City (SoHo). Remote positions available. Ontario, Canada explicitly listed as an eligible location for engineering roles.

Remote policy: Distributed-first since founding. NYC-based employees work from SoHo office 3 days/week (Tue+Thu core days), but engineering roles are available fully remote. Remote engineers can be located in US, Canada, Netherlands, Poland, or Czech Republic.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Yes — verified. Job posting for Engineering Manager, Processing explicitly states: "candidates must be located in the United States, Canada, Netherlands, Poland, or Czech Republic." Ontario, Canada is listed as a location for current engineering openings (Engineering Manager Processing, Software Engineer Treasury). Canada-remote eligibility confirmed for engineering roles.

Engineering & tech: Payments infrastructure: transaction processing at scale, card network integrations (Visa, Mastercard, Amex direct connections), ledger systems. Data Platform role involves data infrastructure/warehousing. Treasury role involves financial account management and money movement. Stack: compliance-grade (SOC 1 Type 1, SOC 2 Type 2, PCI DSS, ISO 27001). Multiple SDK libraries. 99.99%+ uptime guarantee.

Notable / other: Expanded into Canada market in September 2024 (multicurrency processing, first non-US market). Growing fintech infrastructure player. Privacy.com is a sister company. Relatively small team (~193) for the payment volume processed. Engineering roles focused on core payments infrastructure, data platform, and treasury.

Open relevant roles (sample): Senior Software Engineer, Data Platform; Software Engineer, Treasury
