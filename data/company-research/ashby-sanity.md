# Sanity — research
Provider/key: ashby:sanity | company_type: product

What they do: Sanity is a headless content platform that positions itself as an "AI Content Operating System." Companies use Sanity to model their business content structure, automate workflows around it, and deliver content across any channel or application via APIs. Clients include PUMA, Spotify, Figma, Riot Games, and Linear.

How they describe themselves: "The AI Content Operating System – the structured foundation companies use to model how their business works, automate the work around it, and power anything that needs content." Core values: (1) Pioneers — embrace challenges and first-principle thinking; (2) Exist for customers — committed to long-term customer success; (3) "Hel ved" (Norwegian) — trustworthy, genuine, honest communication; (4) "Type 2 Fun" — grow through challenges and persevere together. Tone is developer-focused, startup-rooted, international.

Size / stage / funding: ~288 employees (April 2026), across 6 continents. Raised $180.49M total over 7 rounds. Latest round: Series C for ~$85–87M (May 2025), led by Bullhound Capital with Heavybit, Threshold, ICONIQ Growth, Shopify, Blue Cloud Ventures, and Monochrome Ventures participating.

Locations / HQ: Headquartered in San Francisco, CA and Oslo, Norway. Distributed global team.

Remote policy: Remote-friendly; most roles are listed as remote (21 of 30 open roles are remote, 9 hybrid). The company notes "flexible hours and optional home office / remote in periods" and covers phone and home internet for remote employees.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Unclear — the careers page lists Canada as an eligible location for some roles (Sales, Customer Solutions, People). Engineering roles currently listed (Senior Software Engineer – AI Growth, Senior Software Engineer – Identity, Senior Software Engineer – Content Agent) do not explicitly state Canada on the Ashby board. No explicit exclusion found, but Canada is not confirmed for the current engineering openings. Evidence is mixed.

Engineering & tech: Engineering team organized around product areas (AI Growth, Identity, Content Agent). The platform is API-first, developer-centric. Sanity uses a custom content lake and Studio (React-based editor). Tech leans JavaScript/TypeScript ecosystem. No specific backend infra/platform team surfaced in open roles — current openings are product-feature-oriented.

Notable / other: Sanity pivoted its messaging in 2024–2025 from "headless CMS" to "AI Content OS," reflecting a broader platform play. The company is Norwegian-founded (Oslo) with a US HQ, which may explain the strong European/global remote culture. Well-funded at Series C with a ~$5B secondary valuation rumored.

Open relevant roles (sample): Senior Software Engineer, AI Growth; Senior Software Engineer, Identity; Senior Software Engineer, Content Agent
