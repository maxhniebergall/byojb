# Supabase — research
Provider/key: ashby:supabase | company_type: product

What they do: Supabase is an open-source Firebase alternative providing a hosted Postgres database, authentication, realtime subscriptions, storage, vector embeddings, and edge functions. It serves developers building web and mobile apps who want a full backend-as-a-service with SQL-first architecture. The platform is open source; developers can self-host. Supabase is widely used in the developer community as a rapid backend for startups and enterprises alike.

How they describe themselves: "The open source Firebase alternative." Mission: give developers the power of a full backend without vendor lock-in. Five core values: Egoless (mission over ego), Kaizen Mindset (continuous improvement), Truth Seeking (intellectual honesty, data-driven), Batteries Included (high ownership, high initiative), Undeniable (earn respect through reliability and results). Tone: technically rigorous, pragmatic, globally distributed, low-ego.

Size / stage / funding: 280+ team members across 55+ countries speaking 20+ languages. Raised $500M total. Latest known round: $200M Series D (2024, led by Coatue, with GV, Craft Ventures). Community: 540,000+ members, 1,500+ open-source contributors. Not yet public.

Locations / HQ: No physical offices. Born-remote, globally distributed. Annual in-person off-sites in rotating global locations.

Remote policy: Fully remote, hire globally, no location restrictions, async work with complete schedule flexibility. "Work from anywhere" is the explicit policy. Co-working memberships provided globally. No location-based salary adjustment (pay is not geo-tiered — same pay worldwide per role).
