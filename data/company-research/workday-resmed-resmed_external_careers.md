# Resmed — research
Provider/key: workday:resmed/resmed_external_careers | company_type: product

What they do: ResMed is a global leader in digital health technology and connected medical devices. They develop cloud-connected CPAP machines, ventilators, and software platforms for treating sleep-disordered breathing (sleep apnea), COPD, and other chronic respiratory diseases. Their out-of-hospital software platforms support healthcare professionals and caregivers helping patients manage conditions at home. Over 24.5 million cloud-connected devices; 126 million lives in their digital health network.

How they describe themselves: "Life-changing technology" — their mission is enabling patients to live "full, healthy lives outside of the hospital." They describe themselves as a "global leader in connected care." Their Global Technology Solutions (GTS) division is "dedicated to creating innovative, scalable, and secure platforms and services, with the primary goal of accelerating well-being and growth by transforming the core."

Size / stage / funding: Public company (NYSE: RMD). ~10,000+ employees globally. Sleep and Respiratory Care business earned $4.1B in FY2024, growing 10% YoY. Multi-billion dollar enterprise.

Locations / HQ: San Diego, CA (global HQ). Significant Canada presence — 13+ jobs in Canada (Toronto, Halifax). Platform Engineer roles have been posted for Toronto, ON. Also offices in Australia, Germany, Singapore, India, and elsewhere globally.

Remote policy: Some positions offered with initial remote flexibility ("Remote Initially"). Software Engineer roles have been posted for Canada. Evidence of 21+ remote jobs at ResMed on job boards. Benefits for Canadian employees include RRSP matching, suggesting established Canadian employment infrastructure.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Yes — confirmed. Multiple roles have been posted explicitly for Canada (Toronto, Halifax). Engineering & tech: Cloud infrastructure (AWS primary). Platform engineering involves designing and optimizing cloud infrastructure as code, automation scripts, and enterprise cloud adoption. Python is a key language. Backend engineering is distributed/cloud-native. Stack includes cloud-connected device management at scale, IoT data pipelines. Agile development environment.

Notable / other: ResMed benefits package for Canada includes RRSP matching, stock options, generous vacation, paid volunteer days. Large established company with strong focus on health tech. Their GTS division specifically focuses on platform/cloud engineering as an enabler for the broader product. Not a startup — stable, low-chaos by nature.
