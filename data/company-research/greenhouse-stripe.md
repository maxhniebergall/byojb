# Stripe — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:stripe | company_type: product

What they do: Stripe is a global financial infrastructure platform that provides APIs and tools enabling businesses to accept payments, manage subscriptions, handle payouts, and run financial operations. Their platform powers payment processing, fraud prevention, billing, tax, and financial data infrastructure for millions of businesses worldwide.

How they describe themselves: "The new standard in online payments." Stripe describes itself as economic infrastructure for the internet. More than 8,000 employees ("Stripes") work from offices in 24 cities and remote locations worldwide. Emphasis on craft, long-term thinking, and building with high engineering standards.

Size / stage / funding: ~8,000+ employees. Private company (late-stage, previously valued at ~$65B pre-IPO in 2023). Founded 2010 by Patrick and John Collison. HQ in South San Francisco and Dublin.

Locations / HQ: HQ in South San Francisco, CA. Offices in New York, Seattle, Dublin, London, Singapore, Tokyo, and others. Maintains a "remote hub" — fifth engineering hub — for distributed engineers.

Remote policy: Supports both office-based and fully remote engineers. Remote workers are expected to be 35+ miles / 56+ km from a Stripe office. In-office visits for team events/onsites are welcomed but not required for day-to-day. Roles explicitly tagged "remote" are available.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Yes — verified. There is a dedicated "Backend Engineer, Core Tech, Canada" posting with a Canadian salary range (CA$135,200–CA$202,800). Multiple Canada-based listings appear on LinkedIn (154 Stripe jobs in Canada). Jobs.ca lists remote roles with Canadian salary bands. The Core Technology and Data Platform roles are explicitly available remotely in Canada.

Engineering & tech: Extremely deep infrastructure engineering. Core Infrastructure: Compute, Networking, DocumentDB, distributed caching, high-assurance systems (tier0). Data Infrastructure: data warehouse analytics, streaming analytics, search (Trino/Presto, Apache Pinot, Hive Metastore, Elasticsearch). Developer Infrastructure: inner dev loop, CI/CD, developer productivity. Primary languages: Go, Java, C/C++, Ruby. Large-scale distributed systems at massive scale.

Notable / other: Handles hundreds of billions in annual payment volume. Engineering culture has a reputation for high standards and strong IC career paths. Remote engineering hub established since 2019 (100+ remote hires announced at the time). Roles span payments, risk, billing/tax, core platform, and data.

Open relevant roles (sample): Backend/API Engineer (Money as a Service, Payouts, AI Security, Payments and Risk, Billing/Tax), Backend Engineer Core Tech Canada, Backend Engineer Core Technology, Backend Engineer Data
