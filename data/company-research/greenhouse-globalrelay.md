How they describe themselves: Fosters an environment dedicated to secure compliance solutions built through internal innovation. They emphasize a balance of team collaboration in-office and remote flexibility, highlighting active progression paths and competitive compensation structures.
Size / stage / funding: Established, private, profitable, ~500-1,000 employees.
Locations / HQ: Headquartered in Vancouver, British Columbia, with offices in London, New York, and Halifax.
Remote policy: Hybrid work model requiring a mix of in-office and work-from-home, typically specifying 2+ days a week in the Vancouver or Halifax offices.
Remote-Canada eligibility: Limited. While they are a Canadian company, they require office proximity for hybrid work in Vancouver or Halifax, rather than supporting fully remote roles from anywhere in Canada.
