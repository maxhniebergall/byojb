# Greenbox Capital — research
Provider/key: bamboohr:greenboxcapital | company_type: product

What they do: Greenbox Capital is an alternative small business lender based in Miami, FL. They provide merchant cash advances, invoice factoring, short-term small business loans, equipment financing, and collateral loans to small and medium-sized businesses across the US, Puerto Rico, and Canada. They market themselves as a technology-driven "direct funder" — meaning they lend their own capital rather than brokering loans. Their proprietary platform "The Box" drives underwriting and decisioning.

How they describe themselves: "Provides fast, flexible financing solutions to small and medium-sized businesses across the United States" through technology and data-driven decision-making. They emphasize speed (loans in 24 hours, up to $500K) and accessibility for businesses that can't access traditional bank credit.

Size / stage / funding: Private, bootstrapped/self-funded (no disclosed VC rounds). Founded 2012, Miami FL. ~81–90 employees. Revenue ~$6–20M (sources vary). Small, stable company — not a VC-backed growth startup.

Locations / HQ: Miami, Florida (HQ). Fully remote workforce — no physical office. Also operates Greenboxcapital.ca (Canadian market presence).

Remote policy: Fully remote. "All employees are remote, with no physical office location." Benefits include medical/dental/vision (50% company-paid), 401(k) with 5% match, unlimited PTO.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Unclear / likely US-only. Job postings list "Remote (US)" or "United States Remote" explicitly. The "Lead Data Engineer" posting specifies "Remote (US)." The "Senior Data Engineer" posting from Built In states "Fully remote within the U.S., working Eastern Time hours." Greenbox does serve Canadian customers but data engineering roles appear US-resident only.

Engineering & tech: Azure-heavy stack. Languages: Python, SQL, Salesforce Apex. Databases: Microsoft SQL Server, Delta Lake. Frameworks: Apache Spark (PySpark). Cloud: Microsoft Azure (ADF, Databricks, Azure Data Lake Storage, Cosmos DB, Azure SQL, Databricks Unity Catalog). CRM: Salesforce, HubSpot. Collaboration: Microsoft Teams. Small data engineering team with high ownership scope.

Notable / other: FinTech alternative lending vertical — not high-growth tech startup but stable, profitable. Company has sponsored H1B visas historically. Canadian market presence (greenboxcapital.ca) but hiring appears restricted to US residents. The Eastern Time zone requirement on data roles is a specific callout.

Open relevant roles (sample): Principal Data Engineer; Senior Data Platform Engineer
