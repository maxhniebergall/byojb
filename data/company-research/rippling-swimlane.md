# Swimlane — research
Provider/key: rippling:swimlane | company_type: product

What they do: Swimlane is a security automation and orchestration (SOAR) company providing agentic AI automation for security operations centers (SOC). Their hyperautomation platform enables enterprises and MSSPs to automate threat detection, incident response, vulnerability management, compliance audits, and IT/OT operations. Serves 40+ Fortune 500 companies and 26 federal agencies.

How they describe themselves: "We empower enterprises and MSSPs to prevent breaches and ensure compliance across your entire security organization using agentic AI automation that's built by practitioners for practitioners." Core values: "Punch Above Your Weight Class," "Happy Innovators," "Honesty and Integrity in All the Things," "Move at the Speed of WOW," "Always be Leveling up." Positions itself as "the largest and fastest-growing pure-play security automation company."

Size / stage / funding: ~170 employees. Founded 2015 (Denver, CO). Raised $70M growth round in 2022 + $45M in 2025, approaching profitability. Named Inc. 500 Best Workplaces 2025. Won 2026 CSO Award for Automated SaaS Audit Readiness.

Locations / HQ: Headquarters in Louisville/Denver, Colorado. Staff spans 28 states and 16-24 countries. Remote-first company.

Remote policy: Explicitly remote-first. "Typical time on-site: None." Hybrid and remote employees across the globe. However, open positions found are restricted to United States, United Kingdom, India, and Saudi Arabia — no Canada-eligible roles in current listings.

Remote-Canada eligibility: No/Unclear — despite remote-first claims, current job board listings (Rippling ATS) show no Canada locations. Open positions are US (remote), UK (London), India, and Saudi Arabia. No Canadian listings found.

Engineering & tech: Tech stack: C#, Python, TypeScript; frameworks: Node.js, Angular, ASP.NET Core; DB: MongoDB. Platform integrates with SIEM, endpoint protection, threat intelligence, and cloud security tools. CI/CD roles involve building standardized container images and pipelines. DevOps/CICD Engineer role on ATS targets internal platform engineering.
