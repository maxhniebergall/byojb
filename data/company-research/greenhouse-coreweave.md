# CoreWeave — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:coreweave | company_type: product

What they do: CoreWeave is an AI-native cloud platform specializing in GPU compute infrastructure. They operate the "Essential Cloud for AI," providing enterprises and AI labs with NVIDIA GPU compute (Blackwell, Hopper, Ada Lovelace), CPU compute, bare metal servers, high-performance networking, storage, Kubernetes management, and runtime acceleration tools purpose-built for AI training, inference, and agentic AI workloads.

How they describe themselves: CoreWeave positions itself as "foundational infrastructure" for the next wave of AI, delivering "a cloud platform of cutting-edge software powering the next wave of AI." The company frames its mission around solving critical infrastructure challenges for enterprise AI deployment, with an emphasis on reliability, transparency, and performance optimization. Their core product suite spans accelerated compute, networking, storage, and developer tooling.

Size / stage / funding: Publicly traded (Nasdaq: CRWV) since March 2025 IPO at $40/share, raising $1.5B. Valuation estimated at $23B at IPO. ~1,450 employees (~552 in product/tech). Revenue: $5.1B in 2025 (170% YoY growth), guiding $12–13B for 2026. Backed by NVIDIA ($2B private placement Jan 2026). Major contracts with OpenAI (~$22.4B through 2031) and Meta (~$14.2B through 2031). ~32 data centers, 250,000 GPUs.

Locations / HQ: HQ in Livingston, NJ. Additional US offices in Bellevue WA, New York NY, Philadelphia PA, Sunnyvale CA. UK office in London. Dedicated Canada careers page at coreweave.com/careers/ca; Indeed Canada lists Ontario roles; salary ranges quoted in CAD suggest active Canadian hiring.

Remote policy: Advertises "in-office, hybrid, and remote tech jobs." Previously fully remote; since IPO expansion has moved toward hybrid with recruiters pushing for Sunnyvale office presence (3 days/week). Policy appears to vary by team/manager. Some roles listed as remote; others require on-site. Transitional phase as of mid-2025/2026.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Unclear — evidence is mixed. Canada careers page exists (coreweave.com/careers/ca) and CAD-denominated salaries appear in search results, suggesting some roles are open to Canada. However, increased RTO pressure and hybrid push in US offices muddies whether fully remote Canada is reliably available. Individual role verification required.

Engineering & tech: GPU infrastructure software, Kubernetes-based cluster orchestration, distributed systems, data center infrastructure tooling, data infrastructure services, compute architecture. Tech stack centers on Kubernetes, NVIDIA GPU hardware, high-performance networking (InfiniBand, Ethernet), and cloud-native tooling. Engineering org appears to be organized around infrastructure layers: GPU infra, platform/compute, data infrastructure, networking.

Notable / other: Largest AI-related IPO by amount raised in 2025. Weights & Biases is a subsidiary with a separate careers page. Rapid hypergrowth mode — doubled revenue YoY. Heavy financial leverage noted by analysts (raised ~$28B combined equity+debt). Named to various AI infrastructure "to watch" lists. CNBC Disruptor 50 2025.

Open relevant roles (sample): Senior GPU Infrastructure Software Engineer, Senior Platform Engineer II (Compute Services), Senior SRE (Data Infrastructure), Senior Software Engineer (Cluster Orchestration), Senior Software Engineer (Compute Architecture), Senior Software Engineer (Data Center Infrastructure Tooling), Senior Software Engineer (Data Infrastructure Services)
