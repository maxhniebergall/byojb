# Okta — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:okta | company_type: product
What they do: Okta provides cloud-based identity and access management solutions (IAM) for enterprises. They offer a secure identity platform that enables companies to manage access, authentication, and authorization across applications, devices, and APIs (including Auth0 solutions for developer identity).
How they describe themselves: Grounded in the mission to "free everyone to safely use any technology" as "The World's Identity Company." Their corporate culture is shaped by four values: "Love our customers," "Always secure. Always on," "Build and own it," and "Drive what's next." They focus on a high-ownership and highly reliable software development lifecycle.
Size / stage / funding: Public company (NASDAQ: OKTA), established enterprise, ~6,000+ employees.
Locations / HQ: San Francisco, California (HQ) with global office hubs (including Toronto, Canada).
Remote policy: "Dynamic Work" model supporting a mix of remote-first and office hybrid setups.
Remote-Canada eligibility: Verified yes. They actively hire and support remote software engineering roles in Canada (including British Columbia).
Engineering & tech: High focus on system availability, authentication protocols (OAuth, SAML), encryption, and large-scale SaaS platform infrastructure. Tech stack includes Java, Go, Python, AWS, Node.js, and open-source authorization models (like OpenFGA/Fine-Grained Authorization).
Notable / other: Okta hosts an active developer and engineering blog (okta.com/blog/engineering) and maintains the developer-focused Auth0 engineering blog.
Open relevant roles (sample): Senior Platform Engineer; Software Engineer, Identity Engine; Senior Security Software Engineer.
Sources: okta.com, okta.com/careers, okta.com/blog/engineering.
