Their platform serves as the real-time "nervous system" for applications, specifically empowering developers to build low-latency voice, video, and physical AI agent interactions.
How they describe themselves: LiveKit is an engineering-led organization committed to bringing adaptive real-time computing technology to every developer. Their team values a passion for tinkering, technical conviction, and solving core problems rather than superficial customer issues.
