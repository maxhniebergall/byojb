# Delinea — research
Provider/key: ashby:delinea | company_type: product

What they do: Delinea is a cybersecurity company specializing in Privileged Access Management (PAM) and identity security. Their platform provides AI-driven identity security, PAM, and authorization solutions to secure privileged access across hybrid enterprise environments. Products include Secret Server (privileged credential management), Privilege Manager (least privilege endpoint security), Privileged Remote Access (VPN-less browser-based RDP/SSH), and DevOps Secrets Vault. Formed in 2021 from the merger of Thycotic and Centrify (both PAM leaders). Acquired StrongDM (January 2026) to expand infrastructure access management.
