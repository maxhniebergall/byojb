# Roofr — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:roofr | company_type: product

What they do: Roofr is a SaaS platform for roofing businesses. Their integrated suite covers CRM, instant satellite-measurement-based estimates, proposals, invoicing, payments, material ordering, scheduling, and a job board for roofing contractors. Serves 12,000+ roofing contractors across North America. Founded in Toronto, Ontario in 2016 by Richard Nelson (third-generation roofer, 15+ years in roofing) and CTO Kevin Redman.

How they describe themselves: "The trusted foundation for roofing professionals to build their success." Remote-first company that supports employees working "from wherever you work best." Values inclusion and diversity, mental health (monthly "Laundry Days" — paid Fridays off), and genuine work-life separation. Recognized as "Best Workplaces for Mental Wellness – Canada 2025" and "Best Workplaces for Young Talent in Canada 2025" by Great Place to Work. "Strong sense of connection with this welcoming (and exceptionally talented) team" — employee quote.

Size / stage / funding: ~135–280 employees (sources vary; doubled headcount since last raise). Total funding ~$43.9M–$65.4M over 6 rounds. Latest: Series B (January 2025) led by TCV with ABC Supply (largest roofing materials distributor in North America), Crosslink Capital, Euclid Ventures, MGFO, and i2BF. Y Combinator alum (2017).

Locations / HQ: Toronto, Ontario, Canada (founded here). Multinational operation. Remote-first, Canada-primary hiring.

Remote policy: Remote-first. Employees work from wherever they work best. Home office setup support provided. Respects working hours boundaries for genuine personal time separation.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Yes — confirmed. The vast majority of engineering and other roles are explicitly listed as "Remote, Canada." Senior Backend Engineer AI Platform and Senior Analytics Engineer are both Remote Canada. RRSP matching and 100% employer-paid Extended Healthcare and Dental in Canada are listed as benefits. This is a Canadian company hiring primarily in Canada.

Engineering & tech: The AI Platform Backend role involves building application-layer foundations for AI integration — integration patterns, agent scaffolding, shared architecture for LLM-powered features, data pipelines for AI (RAG, embeddings, vector search), structured data feeds, and evaluation pipelines for LLM systems. Stack includes LLM APIs (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI), LangChain or similar agent frameworks, backend systems at scale. The "Senior Backend Engineer - Contract" is Brazil-based (different from the Canada role). Engineering team is small but scaling with Series B investment.

Notable / other: Roofr is a Canadian-founded, Canada-primary remote employer with real growth (Series B 2025, TCV as lead is a credible growth investor). The AI Platform role is application/integration engineering rather than core infrastructure — it's more "backend building AI plumbing" than infra/cloud-ops. The "Senior Analytics Engineer" role is a data/analytics track. Benefits explicitly include Canadian-market perks (RRSP, healthcare, dental).

Open relevant roles (sample): Senior Backend Engineer, AI Platform (Remote, Canada); Senior Backend Engineer - Contract (Remote, Brazil); Engineering Team Lead, AI Platform (Remote, Canada); Senior Analytics Engineer (Remote, Canada)
