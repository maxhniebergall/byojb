# Planet — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:planetlabs | company_type: product

What they do: Planet Labs PBC operates the world's largest fleet of commercial Earth observation satellites (200+ in orbit). Their mission is to "image all of Earth's landmass every day, and make global change visible, accessible and actionable." Planet provides satellite imagery products, analytics, and AI-powered insights across agriculture, forestry, defense & intelligence, government, and commercial applications. Products include PlanetScope (3m daily global), SkySat (0.5m high-resolution), Tanager (hyperspectral), and monitoring platforms.

How they describe themselves: "We seek the greatest good in everything we do." Mission-driven company emphasizing ethical business practices and impact. Team described as "rocket scientists, software engineers, creatives, business strategists, and researchers." Culture emphasizes learning, teaching, and dialogue. Committed to "building a community where everyone belongs."

Size / stage / funding: Public company (NYSE: PL). ~700 employees. HQ San Francisco. Offices in Berlin, Washington DC, Haarlem (NL), Ljubljana (Slovenia), Graz (Austria). Deployed 450+ satellites total; announced Berlin satellite manufacturing expansion in September 2025.

Locations / HQ: San Francisco (HQ), Berlin, Washington DC, Haarlem (NL), Ljubljana (Slovenia), Graz (Austria). Canada is not a primary office location.
