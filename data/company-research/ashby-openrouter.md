# OpenRouter — research
Provider/key: ashby:openrouter | company_type: product

What they do: OpenRouter provides a unified API routing layer for large language models. They aggregate 400+ LLMs from multiple providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Mistral, Meta, etc.) and route developer requests to the optimal endpoint based on price, latency, and uptime. Revenue model: ~5% margin on inference spend. Used by AI developers and enterprises who want model flexibility without vendor lock-in. Essentially an LLM infrastructure utility/aggregator.
