# Ditto — research
Provider/key: ashby:ditto | company_type: product
What they do: Ditto.live develops an edge-native database and peer-to-peer data synchronization platform that allows apps to sync data in real-time without internet access or central servers.
How they describe themselves: Dedicated to "changing the world one peer at a time." They define their culture around three core values: "Trust, Communication, and Continual Improvement." They emphasize peer-to-peer connection among staff and customers.
Size / stage / funding: Private venture-backed startup, established, ~100+ employees.
Locations / HQ: Headquartered in San Francisco, CA, but operates as a distributed team.
Remote policy: Remote-first, fully distributed since 2018.
Remote-Canada eligibility: Unclear to No. Most job listings specify US hiring regions (or specific European timezones), and many public sector / defense integrations require US citizenship and security clearances. There is no evidence of active hiring or employment support for remote workers based in Canada.
Engineering & tech: High focus on P2P networking, replication protocols, database engines, and systems engineering. Heavily Rust-based.
Notable / other: Widely used for offline-capable sync in aviation, stadiums, and defense (Android Team Awareness Kit - ATAK integrations).
Open relevant roles (sample): Systems Engineer, Database Replication (Rust); Platform Engineer.
