# Trust Wallet — research
Provider/key: ashby:trust-wallet | company_type: product

What they do: Trust Wallet is a non-custodial, self-custody cryptocurrency wallet supporting 100+ blockchains and 10+ million digital assets and NFTs. It enables users to store, send, receive, and interact with Web3 applications entirely from their own device without relying on a centralized custodian. Trusted by 200M+ users globally since 2017.

How they describe themselves: "Trust Wallet is the world's most inclusive, chain-agnostic, self-custody wallet." The company emphasizes decentralization, user ownership of keys, and openness across blockchains. Cultural values include "remote and flexible working arrangements," a flat organizational structure, and being "a truly global organization, with international teams." Every open role explicitly states: "This is a global and fully remote role."

Size / stage / funding: Wholly-owned subsidiary of Binance (acquired 2018). Established product with 200M+ users. Part of the Binance ecosystem — not independently funded. Team distributed across 19+ time zones.

Locations / HQ: No fixed HQ — fully distributed global team. Operationally tied to Binance (Cayman Islands-registered, global operations).

Remote policy: Fully remote, global. Every job posting states it is "a global and fully remote role." Employees must have the legal right to work in the country they are based in. Flexible working hours.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Yes (verified). Roles are global; the policy explicitly states you must have the right to work in your own country — Canadians working in Canada are eligible. Multiple engineering roles listed on global remote job boards confirm worldwide eligibility.

Engineering & tech: Backend (Go), Platform Engineering (CI/CD, Kubernetes, Prometheus, Grafana, Sentry), blockchain-specific engineering (Kotlin, Rust, Solidity), mobile (iOS/Android). Engineering org is flat, cross-functional, and distributed. Stack: Go, Kubernetes, Docker, PostgreSQL, various blockchain node integrations. Senior Backend Engineer (Go) role targets blockchain data services and APIs; Senior Platform Engineer role owns infrastructure deployments, CI/CD pipelines, and production observability.

Notable / other: Regulatory environment for crypto companies is an ongoing consideration globally. Binance parent has faced regulatory scrutiny in multiple jurisdictions. Trust Wallet itself operates as a decentralized product (no custody), which provides some regulatory insulation. Flat org, async-friendly culture. Web3/crypto domain expertise expected.
