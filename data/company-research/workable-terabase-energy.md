# Terabase Energy — research
Provider/key: workable:terabase-energy | company_type: product

What they do: Terabase Energy is a climate technology company that builds digital and automation solutions for utility-scale solar construction. They address the full solar project lifecycle with four main offerings: Construct (GIS-native SaaS construction management software — 25 GW of projects using it), Terafab® (robotic/automated construction platform), Engineering (solar energy modeling and design software — PlantPredict, 3 TW simulated across 30,000+ projects), and SCADA (power plant controls and monitoring — 10 GW using their systems). Goal: reduce cost and increase scalability of utility-scale solar globally.

How they describe themselves: Mission "to reduce the cost and increase the scalability of solar by building an interconnected digital and automation platform for the development, construction, and operations of utility-scale PV power plants." Values: unlocking scalability through optimization, game-changing innovation (robotics, IoT), deep industry expertise, and serving as "a true project partner." Named Fast Company World's Most Innovative Companies 2026 and Cleantech Group Global Cleantech 100 2026.

Size / stage / funding: Founded 2019. Series C ($130M, led by SoftBank Vision Fund 2, March 2025). Earlier investors include Breakthrough Energy Ventures, City Light Capital, EDP Energias De Portugal, Fifth Wall, Powerhouse Ventures, Prelude Ventures, US DOE. Total equity ~$205M+. Growth stage. 20+ open roles as of 2026.

Locations / HQ: Berkeley, CA (primary HQ/office). Also Davis CA, Fort Worth TX, Sacramento CA, Toledo OH, Chihuahua Mexico, Paris France. Offices span US and international.

Remote policy: Remote flexibility offered as a benefit. However, specific roles are designated "Remote, United States" — meaning US-based remote, not internationally open.

Remote-Canada eligibility: No — all remote roles explicitly state "Remote, United States." The IT Infrastructure Engineer role is listed as "Sacramento, CA (In-Office or Remote)" and SCADA roles are "Remote, United States." No Canadian hiring found. Company is US-domiciled and appears to restrict remote eligibility to US residents.

Engineering & tech: SaaS platform (Construct), SCADA/control systems, GIS software, IoT/robotics integration, cloud infra. IT Infrastructure role involves IT systems management (not DevOps/platform scale). SCADA/Ignition developer role requires industrial controls expertise. Stack not fully disclosed; assumed cloud-based web platform.

Notable / other: One of the most credibly innovative cleantech companies — strong mission, impressive investors, real product traction (25 GW, 3 TW). Rapid growth post-Series C. However all technical roles are US-specific and involve a mix of office/field presence. The IT Infrastructure role is operational IT (not cloud platform infra). SCADA role is specialized industrial controls. Neither maps cleanly to backend/platform/distributed-systems IC work.

Open relevant roles (sample): IT Infrastructure Engineer (Sacramento CA / Remote); Senior Software Developer - SCADA / Ignition
