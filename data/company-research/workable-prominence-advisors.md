# Prominence Advisors — research
Provider/key: workable:prominence-advisors | company_type: consulting

What they do: Prominence Advisors is a healthcare data consulting and solutions firm founded in 2011 by former Epic leaders. They specialize in data enablement for large healthcare systems — helping hospitals and health networks "do more with their data" through analytics, data architecture, AI/ML, governance, and Epic EHR services. They describe themselves as "THE DATA ENABLEMENT COMPANY." Service areas: Organize (governance/strategy), Architect (cloud/data infrastructure), Model (ML/analytics), Visualize (dashboards), Operationalize (managed services/staffing). Proprietary products include Extraction Console, Configuration Console, and Data Prep Engine.

How they describe themselves: "Empowering health systems across the country with self-service analytics since 2011." Won 2x Best in KLAS award. Six core principles: honesty, judgment, curiosity, humility, courage, drive. They emphasize work-life balance, "family-friendly flexibility," individualized career paths, and mission-driven healthcare impact. Named "Top 10 Best Small Firm to Work For" by Consulting Magazine.

Size / stage / funding: Founded 2011, Chicago IL. Employee count not disclosed publicly (~50-200 range implied by "small firm" awards and scale of operations). Private, no disclosed VC funding. Acquired Tegra Analytics (September 2025) to enter life sciences. Also acquired WLH Consulting & Learning Solutions. Revenue not disclosed publicly. Long-running established firm.

Locations / HQ: Chicago, Illinois (HQ). Fully remote workforce distributed across the US. "Staff averages 50-95% remote work." Awards for "Best Remote & Hybrid Workplaces in America."

Remote policy: Highly remote-friendly (50-95% remote). Team meets at periodic in-person summits. Noted as winners of Best Remote & Hybrid Workplaces in America.

Remote-Canada eligibility: No — roles require "legally authorized to work in the United States without sponsorship." Job postings specify "Remote Nationwide" meaning US-only. No Canadian operations or hiring mentioned. Their consulting work is exclusively for US healthcare organizations.

Engineering & tech: Data engineering (Epic EHR extraction, SQL, Python), Azure, Databricks, Snowflake, AWS, Tableau, Informatica, Collibra, Microsoft. Data pipelines and healthcare analytics infrastructure. Partners with all major data platform vendors. Roles involve building pipelines for healthcare providers.

Notable / other: Consulting firm, not a product company — engineers work on client engagements across multiple health systems, which means client-driven work and potential travel. "Staff augmentation" is listed as a service line (placement consulting). Growing through acquisitions into life sciences. Good reputation in healthcare IT niche. Strong culture indicators.

Open relevant roles (sample): Data Engineer; Principal Data Platform Engineer (Databricks)
