# VRChat — research
Provider/key: lever:vrchat | company_type: product

What they do: VRChat is one of the largest social virtual reality platforms in the world. Users create avatars and 3D worlds and meet in them, accessible on VR headsets or desktop. The platform hosts 250,000+ user-created worlds and millions of users. The company has an emerging economy layer — creator monetization, subscriptions, digital ownership, payment processing, and economic analytics — which is the focus of the Backend Engineer Economy role.

How they describe themselves: "Bring billions of people together through immersive social experiences that transcend physical barriers." Five core values: Be Bold (innovation/experimentation), Debate and Commit (diverse viewpoints then unified action), Better Together (collaborative respect), Own Outcomes (take responsibility), and For VRChat (prioritize collective success). A "remote-first company, with a team spread across the globe" that values flexibility, trust, and autonomy.

Size / stage / funding: Private company. ~166 employees (51-200 range). Total funding: ~$100M (Anthos Capital, Gravity Fund, Makers Fund, GFR Fund, Brightstone Venture Capital, HTC, and others — 8 investors total). Founded 2014. The company reports the team is distributed across the United States, Canada, and Europe.

Locations / HQ: No fixed HQ — 100% remote. Team distributed across the US, Canada, and Europe. Every role is tagged "Anywhere" on the Lever job board. Company headquartered in Los Angeles, CA for legal/administrative purposes.

Remote policy: 100% remote, tags every open role "Anywhere." No country restrictions identified. Globally distributed team including confirmed Canadian team members. Flexible, async-friendly culture.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Yes (confirmed). Company explicitly lists Canada as a location where team members work. All roles tagged "Anywhere." Remote-first with global team including US, Canada, and Europe.

Engineering & tech: Real-time social VR infrastructure, backend APIs (Node.js/TypeScript for Economy), platform engineering (reliability, observability, scalability, incident response), game backend systems. Senior/Staff Platform Engineer role: improving reliability/performance/scalability of production platform, operating infrastructure, driving observability and incident response. Senior/Staff Backend Engineer (Economy) role: building subscriptions, digital ownership, creator monetization, payment processing, and economic analytics systems — high-integrity backend infrastructure at global scale. Stack: Node.js, TypeScript, Kubernetes, cloud infrastructure (AWS inferred), Prometheus/Grafana for observability.

Notable / other: VRChat is a metaverse company predating the "metaverse hype" wave, with an organic, creator-driven community. The Economy layer is a newer, infrastructure-heavy initiative. Unlimited PTO policy. The team spans US, Canada, and Europe — genuinely international. 100M+ total funding. Currently hiring for Senior/Staff Platform Engineer and Senior/Staff Backend Engineer (API) — Economy.
