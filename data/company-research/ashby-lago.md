# Lago — research
Provider/key: ashby:lago | company_type: product

What they do: Lago is an open-source billing infrastructure platform providing usage metering, subscription management, billing/invoicing, entitlements management, payment orchestration, and revenue analytics. It is positioned as an open-source alternative to Stripe Billing and Chargebee, targeting companies with complex or usage-based pricing models. Notable customers include Mistral AI, PayPal, and 1NCE. Available as self-hosted (open source) and as a managed cloud product.

How they describe themselves: "The billing platform that gives teams full transparency, control and flexibility to manage and scale any pricing model." Also describe themselves as "The AI Native Billing Platform." Team culture quote: "My favorite part is that we have a culture of taking ownership: you can trust that everyone takes responsibility." The company values are accountability, ownership, reliability, and proactivity. They describe themselves as "a team of ambitious, focused individuals who work together to solve hard problems with excellence and accountability."

Size / stage / funding: Founded 2021, Paris. Y Combinator S21 cohort. Raised $22.125M total: seed from YC + FirstMark Capital + SignalFire + individual angels (Meghan Gill/MongoDB monetization lead, Romain Huet/ex-Stripe, Clément Delangue/HuggingFace CEO). Team size: ~23–67 people (small and growing). HQ: Paris, France, with plans for international expansion including US go-to-market.

Locations / HQ: Paris, France (headquarters). International remote team for engineering. Americas-timezone positions (Canada + Brazil) listed.

Remote policy: Remote-friendly. Explicit "Remote Canada" and "Remote Canada, Brazil" positions listed on their jobs page for Infrastructure Engineer and Senior Backend Engineer roles.

Remote-Canada eligibility: YES — verified. Job board listings (YC job board) explicitly show "Infrastructure Engineer (CA)" and "Senior Backend Engineer (CA/BR)" with locations listed as Remote Canada and Remote Canada/Brazil respectively.

Engineering & tech: Ruby on Rails (API/billing engine), PostgreSQL, React (frontend). The billing engine is open source on GitHub (getlago/lago, 9,785+ stars). Backend engineers work on the core billing engine and API. Infrastructure engineers focus on scalability, reliability, and deployment infrastructure. Small team means broad scope.

Notable / other: 9,785+ GitHub stars on the open-source repo. Backed by operators from Stripe, MongoDB, and HuggingFace — strong developer-tools/API-economy network. Self-described as "AI native billing" in 2025–26, likely integrating AI pricing/metering use cases. Founding team is French; company is incorporated internationally.

Open relevant roles (sample): Infrastructure Engineer, Senior Backend Engineer
