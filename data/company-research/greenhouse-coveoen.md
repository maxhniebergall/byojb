# Coveo — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:coveoen | company_type: product

What they do: Coveo is an enterprise AI-powered search and experience platform. They provide AI search, recommendations, personalization, and generative answering capabilities for commerce, customer service, workplace, and website use cases. The platform processes 25+ billion events monthly and releases 1,500+ automatic cloud updates monthly. Customers include large enterprise brands across retail, financial services, high-tech, and government sectors.

How they describe themselves: "We help enterprises become business-to-person and thrive in the experience economy." They state that "only AI can enhance individualization and business outcomes simultaneously, at a global, enterprise scale." ~40% of their ~823-person team is R&D, data science, and engineering. Coveo positions itself as a research-intensive AI company, not a commoditized vendor. They recently signed an MOU with the Government of Canada to advance AI innovation in the public service (December 2025).

Size / stage / funding: Publicly traded on the TSX (ticker: CVO). IPO raised $169.81M in November 2021. Post-IPO follow-on funding in July 2025. Total raised ~$576M across 9 rounds. ~823 employees as of February 2026. Founded 2005. HQ in Quebec City / Montreal.

Locations / HQ: Headquartered in Quebec City and Montreal, Quebec, Canada. Additional offices globally. Canada-based company.

Remote policy: Hybrid model — roles listed are typically Quebec-province based (Montreal, Quebec City, Sherbrooke). Some roles listed as hybrid/in-person. Remote roles exist (e.g., DevSecOps Specialist listed as remote) but most engineering roles are Quebec-specific. Data Platform Infrastructure role is listed for Montreal.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Partial — they are a Canadian company and roles are in Canada, but most engineering roles are Quebec-city/office-anchored rather than fully remote across Canada. A candidate in BC (Mountain Time) would need to assess whether specific roles allow full remote outside Quebec or require hybrid in Quebec offices.

Engineering & tech: Stack includes AWS, Snowflake, Databricks, Terraform, Kafka, Apache Spark, Delta Lake, Python, SQL. Platform engineering team manages data platform infrastructure at scale. DevOps/tooling team uses CI/CD, Kubernetes, infrastructure-as-code. Large R&D org (~40% of company) with significant data science and ML investment.

Notable / other: Gartner Magic Quadrant leader for AI-powered search and product discovery. Publicly traded, Canadian company, strong government relationships. One of Canada's established enterprise AI companies. Recent MOU with Government of Canada for public service AI modernization.

Open relevant roles (sample): Senior DevOps - Data Platform Infrastructure; Senior Software Developer, Data Platform Infrastructure (Montreal, QC)
