# OpenAI — research
Provider/key: ashby:openai | company_type: product

What they do: OpenAI is the creator of GPT-4, ChatGPT, DALL-E, Sora, Codex, and the o-series reasoning models. They develop foundational large language models and multimodal AI systems. Products include ChatGPT (consumer), the OpenAI API platform (developers/enterprises), and Operator/agent systems. Their stated mission is "ensuring artificial general intelligence benefits all of humanity." They operate Stargate, a major US AI infrastructure initiative with government and enterprise backing.

How they describe themselves: Mission: "ensure that artificial general intelligence benefits all of humanity." Strong safety and alignment focus (though debated externally). Culture described as fast-moving, ambitious, collaborative but highly competitive internally. CEO Sam Altman has explicitly stated remote work is "a mistake" and has pushed for office-first culture.

Size / stage / funding: ~3,000+ employees. Valued at $300B+ (2025 fundraise). Backed by Microsoft (major strategic investor, $13B+), SoftBank ($30B Stargate commitment), Thrive Capital, and others. Revenue run rate reported at $3.4B+ in 2024, growing rapidly. Not yet public.

Locations / HQ: Headquarters in San Francisco, CA. Offices in New York, London, Dublin, Tokyo, Paris, Singapore, Seattle, Sydney, Seoul, Washington DC. No Canadian office.

Remote policy: Hybrid/office-first. Standard expectation is 3 in-office days per week (Mon–Wed). Only 28 of 728 open roles (~4%) are designated remote — the lowest proportion among comparable AI labs. Most remote roles are India-only (Communications Manager, Consumer Marketing Manager, AI Deployment Manager). CEO explicitly does not support full remote. There is no global work-from-anywhere model at OpenAI.

Remote-Canada eligibility: NO — not eligible. OpenAI has no Canadian office and no remote-Canada roles listed. International remote roles confirmed only for India-specific positions. All engineering roles reviewed specify San Francisco or other US office locations.

Engineering & tech: Python (training, data pipelines), C++, CUDA (model infra), Go, Kubernetes, internal distributed systems at extreme scale. Teams include RL Training Infra, Data Infrastructure, Developer Productivity, Observability, Collective Communication (GPU networking), Scaled Abuse, Data Acquisition. Very high scale (training runs, inference infra, data pipelines).

Notable / other: Sam Altman famously called remote work "a mistake" in 2023; policy has not materially changed. OpenAI is deeply office-culture despite being a tech company. Stargate is a multi-hundred-billion-dollar US AI infrastructure initiative. Partnership with Microsoft, US government contracts. Company is under significant regulatory scrutiny globally. Very competitive hiring process (often cited as among the most rigorous in tech).

Open relevant roles (sample): Software Engineer - RL Training Infra, Data Engineer, Software Engineer - Data Infrastructure, Software Engineer - Developer Productivity, Software Engineer - Observability, Software Engineer - Collective Communication
