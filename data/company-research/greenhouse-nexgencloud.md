# NexGen Cloud — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:nexgencloud | company_type: product

What they do: NexGen Cloud is a UK-based AI cloud infrastructure provider offering GPU-as-a-service. Their main products are: (1) Hyperstack — a self-serve GPUaaS platform with on-demand NVIDIA HGX/H100/GB200 GPUs and managed Kubernetes; (2) Secure Private Cloud — single-tenant HPC with customized hardware; (3) Infrahub Compute — physical compute financing and deployment. They position themselves as "sovereign" AI infrastructure (non-US-jurisdiction, EU-compliant), targeting enterprises and AI researchers who need GDPR-safe GPU compute.

How they describe themselves: "The Enterprise AI Factory" — delivering "customised solutions for every workload, at any scale." "UK-owned and EU-sovereign." Emphasizes diversity, collaboration, curiosity, and ownership. Culture described as "high-performing team where curiosity fuels innovation." Benefits include 25 days PTO, flexible/remote working, real ownership and autonomy, and clear career progression.

Size / stage / funding: ~107 employees (January 2026). Revenue grew from $3M (2023) to $70M+ (end 2024), with a 380% AI cloud revenue increase. Raised $45M Series A (April 2025) at a $354M valuation. Total funding ~$64.4M. Lead investors not publicly named beyond press release.

Locations / HQ: London, UK (HQ, 9-13 St Andrew St). Marbella, Spain (second office). Team members also in India and Portugal. Data centers in Europe and Canada (renewable energy powered).

Remote policy: "We hire for both remote and in-office positions, depending on the team's need, structure and department." Remote roles confirmed for UK and Australia. Most London roles are in-office.

Remote-Canada eligibility: No evidence — all engineering roles currently listed are UK-Remote or Australia-Remote. Despite having Canadian data center infrastructure, no Canada-based remote engineering positions are currently posted. The job board shows: Senior Infrastructure Engineer (UK - Remote), Senior Infrastructure Engineer (Australia - Remote), HPC Cluster Architect (UK - Remote). No Canada-eligible engineering openings.

Engineering & tech: Stack for the Senior Infrastructure Engineer role: OpenStack (production-essential), Kubernetes at scale, Linux, networking and storage systems, IaC and GitOps practices, GPU workload optimization (NVIDIA tools), CI/CD and Git workflows. The platform serves GPU-intensive workloads for AI researchers and enterprises. Engineering focus is on OpenStack+Kubernetes cloud infrastructure, which is specialized and technically demanding.

Notable / other: NexGen Cloud is a fast-growing early-stage company (Series A, 107 employees) with strong revenue momentum. The "sovereign AI" angle differentiates them from AWS/Azure/GCP. The Senior Infrastructure Engineer role in Australia is the specific batch role — interesting for its OpenStack/GPU focus but geographically wrong for Canada.

Open relevant roles (sample): Senior Infrastructure Engineer (Australia - Remote); Senior Infrastructure Engineer (UK - Remote); HPC Cluster Architect (UK - Remote)
