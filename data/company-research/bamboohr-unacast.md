# Unacast — research
Provider/key: bamboohr:unacast | company_type: product

What they do: Unacast is a location intelligence and human mobility data company. They collect, process, and sell location data derived from 1B+ monthly mobile devices to help businesses understand physical-world behavior. Products include: Global Location Data, Foot Traffic Data (commercial points of interest), Location Insights Platform (AI-powered US location analysis), Consumer Data Enrichment (identity graph/resolution), and Telco Data Processing (raw location → privacy-friendly mobility insights). Clients span retail, real estate, supply chain, telecommunications, adtech, government, and consulting (partners include Google, BCG, Harvard, LiveRamp).

How they describe themselves: Inspired by Norwegian work philosophy ("Work Like a Norwegian"). Three core values: "We Build Trust" (transparency, openness), "We Win as a Team" (collective success), "We Own It" (pride in work, embracing learning). Tone is balanced, data-first, Scandinavian work-culture influenced. They emphasize happiness, trust, and work-life balance.

Size / stage / funding: Founded Norway (Oslo + Ashburn VA HQ). ~77–78 employees. Revenue ~$8.5M. Total funding raised: ~$103M (Crunchbase/PitchBook). Latest funding: $28M debt round (December 2025) from Vector Capital (private equity).

Locations / HQ: Ashburn, Virginia, USA (primary HQ). Norwegian roots. Hybrid/remote across US, with stated preference for EST-timezone candidates and specific US states (CT, FL, GA, MD, NC, NJ, NY, PA, RI, SC, VA).

Remote policy: Hybrid/flexible. "Currently working remotely and offering a hybrid solution where possible." Remote available but EST timezone preference and US-state residency requirements noted.

Remote-Canada eligibility: No — current roles ("Software Engineer - Core Platform" and "Senior Software Engineer - APIs") list "Ashburn, VA, USA" as location and specify "In-Office or Remote" with US-state residency requirements. EST timezone preference. No mention of Canada-eligible positions. Salary listed in USD for US workers.

Engineering & tech: Processing "tens of billions of location signal data points each day." Backend services for data pipelines, batch processing, and APIs. Technology stack not fully disclosed publicly, but scale suggests distributed data processing (likely Go/Python, big data infra). Two engineering roles open: Core Platform (batch/data pipelines) and APIs (customer-facing and internal).

Notable / other: Interesting technical problem domain (mobility data at scale). Norwegian culture influence on work style. Took on $28M debt in late 2025 — may signal cash constraints. Company is relatively small (~$8.5M revenue) for its funding level ($103M total). US-centric hiring despite international roots.
