# Forma.ai — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:formaaiinc | company_type: product

What they do: Forma.ai is a sales performance management (SPM) platform focused on incentive compensation management, territory and quota planning, and sales analytics. They describe themselves as "the world's first sales compensation solution driven by a collective data model," using AI and data science to optimize sales incentive spending. Customers are enterprise B2B companies managing complex sales compensation plans. Made their first acquisition (SeaMonster, Toronto) expanding into territory management.

How they describe themselves: "Employees can work from where they are most productive, whether that's in our Toronto HQ, from home, or a combination of both." Named one of Canada's fastest-growing companies; Deloitte Fast 50 Canada winner 2024. Tone is scale-up/growth company. Values emphasize data-driven decision making and customer outcomes.

Size / stage / funding: Series B company. Raised $57.8M CAD ($45M USD) Series B in April 2022 led by ACME Capital, with Crosslink Capital, Golden Ventures, Uncork Capital, Xfund, Gaingels. Total ~$58M raised across 3 rounds. ~140-170 employees. Founded 2016. HQ Toronto, Ontario, Canada.

Locations / HQ: Headquarters in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Engineering team primarily Toronto-based. Additional operations team in Pune, India (hybrid).

Remote policy: Flexible hybrid — "employees can work from where they are most productive, whether that's in our Toronto HQ, from home, or a combination of both." Most engineering and leadership roles are listed as Toronto or "Remote Canada" / "Remote Canada or Remote USA" on specific postings. Some roles explicitly state "Remote Canada; Remote USA."

Remote-Canada eligibility: Yes — confirmed. Roles explicitly list "Remote Canada" as an option. Toronto HQ but remote-Canada hiring is supported for some positions. A BC-based candidate would likely qualify for roles labeled "Remote Canada."

Engineering & tech: Backend engineering for sales compensation calculation engine, data pipelines, and data platform. Stack includes JavaScript, React, Python, Django, Postgres, AWS. Data platform role involves big data pipelines and analytics. Staff-level backend roles involve applications and data platform work. ~4-5 engineering roles currently open at senior/staff level.

Notable / other: Well-funded Canadian scale-up in a niche but mature SaaS vertical (sales comp). Stable product with clear enterprise customer base. First acquisition (SeaMonster) suggests M&A-driven growth path. Recognized by Deloitte Fast 50 Canada. Not AI hype — AI is applied to compensation modeling, a concrete business problem.

Open relevant roles (sample): Staff Back End Engineer (Applications) (Toronto / Remote Canada); Staff Back End Engineer (Data Platform) (Toronto / Remote Canada)
