# Railway — research
Provider/key: ashby:railway | company_type: product

What they do: Railway is a cloud infrastructure and deployment platform. "Infrastructure, instantly." Founded 2020 by Jake Cooper. Allows developers to provision databases, services, and applications in the cloud with minimal configuration, git-native tooling, auto-scaling, monitoring, and security. They moved from PaaS-on-GCP (2020–2024) to owning their own co-location data centers (2024+). 2M+ users, shipping 20K+ new applications daily, 100K paid users across 25K businesses (including Fortune 500).

How they describe themselves: "We rebuilt the cloud computing stack so developers can focus on shipping software." Four core values: honorable, accelerating progress through shipping quickly, reliability through ownership, directness in communication. Prioritize "compounding small wins" and moving fast. "Autonomous and async heavy, happy with engineers working anywhere." Small, fully remote team.

Size / stage / funding: ~57 employees. Total funding: $120M ($20M Series A 2022 led by Redpoint Ventures; $100M raised in later round per 2025/2026 reports). Angel investors include Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch and GitHub co-founder Tom Preston-Werner. Founded 2020 (Jake Cooper, ex-Wolfram, ex-Uber Infrastructure).

Locations / HQ: San Francisco, CA (registered). Fully remote/distributed globally.

Remote policy: Fully remote. "Work from anywhere." First-class hardware provided. Unlimited PTO. Async-heavy. Biannual team retreats. No geographic restrictions stated.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Yes — likely confirmed. Job posting for "Senior Infra Engineer: Platform" explicitly states "Remote (anywhere)" with no geographic restrictions. The company's stated hiring policy is globally distributed and async. No evidence of Canada exclusion found.

Engineering & tech: Deep infrastructure work: Golang, gRPC, PostgreSQL, eBGP/eBPF, Wireguard, Vector, VictoriaMetrics, Clickhouse, ZFS, Ceph, VirtioFS, BuildKit, SOCI, Zot, overlayfs. Bare-metal orchestration, storage systems, observability, datacenter networking. Roles span: Infrastructure, Infra - Datacenters, Platform Engineering (Storage), Observability, Bare-metal Orchestration.

Notable / other: Raised $100M in 2025/2026 to disrupt major cloud providers. Moving into own co-location data centers is a significant technical leap. Very deep low-level infrastructure work — closest thing to "build the cloud" in the startup world. Small team (~57 people) means high ownership/low bureaucracy but potentially high-chaos during rapid expansion. On-call rotation required (1-2 weeks quarterly per job posting).

Open relevant roles (sample): Infrastructure Engineer; Infra Engineer - Datacenters; Senior Platform Engineer: Storage; Senior Infra Engineer: Observability; Senior Infra Engineer: Baremetal Orchestration
