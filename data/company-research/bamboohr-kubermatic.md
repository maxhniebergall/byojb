# Kubermatic — research
Provider/key: bamboohr:kubermatic | company_type: product

What they do: Kubermatic is a Hamburg-based cloud-native software company that builds tools to fully automate Kubernetes and cloud-native operations at enterprise scale. Their platform manages thousands of Kubernetes clusters across multi-cloud, hybrid, and edge/on-prem environments. Key products: Kubermatic Kubernetes Platform (multi-cluster management), KubeLB (multi-tenant load balancing), Kubermatic Virtualization (private cloud), Kubermatic Developer Platform (developer productivity), and Kubermatic SecureGuard (secrets management). They are also a core contributor to the CNCF kcp project (a Kubernetes Sandbox project).

How they describe themselves: "Empower organizations worldwide to fully automate their Kubernetes and cloud native operations across multi-cloud, edge and on-prem." Core values: getting things done, innovation, valuing differences, optimism, treating customers as partners, and community-driven approaches. Tone is technical, open-source oriented, enterprise-focused. They emphasize flat hierarchy, continuous learning, inclusive culture, and flexible working.

Size / stage / funding: Founded 2016, Hamburg Germany. ~51–53 employees. Total funding ~$12.6M (multiple seed rounds, led by Nauta Capital and btov Partners). Revenue ~$3.5M (2024). Named in Gartner Magic Quadrant for Container Management and Forrester Wave Q3 2025 for Multicloud Container Platforms. Key customers: Lufthansa, Bosch, T-Systems, Siemens, Swisscom, Allianz, Vonage.

Locations / HQ: Hamburg, Germany (HQ). Globally distributed remote team. Office in Hamburg for hybrid.

Remote policy: Fully remote-first. "Working from home, at the beach, in the mountains, at our office in the beautiful city of Hamburg or hybrid – it's your choice!" Globally distributed team.

Remote-Canada eligibility: No — roles are explicitly Europe-only (CET ±4h timezone). Both the "Open Source Software Engineer (kcp)" and "Product Software Engineer (Kubermatic Dev Platform)" roles are listed as Germany-only or Europe (CET ±4h). Canada (Mountain Time, UTC-6/7) falls far outside this window.

Engineering & tech: Kubernetes, Go (Golang), Rust, cloud-native operators/controllers, CNCF ecosystem, CI/CD automation, multi-cloud (AWS/GCP/Azure/on-prem). Engineering organized around open-source projects and enterprise product development.

Notable / other: Strong open-source presence (kcp CNCF Sandbox, KKP). Enterprise clients are marquee European companies. Company is intentionally Germany/Europe-centric in hiring despite claiming global remote. Roles in North America were not found in search results.

Open relevant roles (sample): Open Source Software Engineer (mid/senior) — kcp (CNCF Sandbox Project); Product Software Engineer (mid/senior) — Kubermatic Dev Platform
