# DoubleVerify — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:doubleverify | company_type: product
What they do: DoubleVerify provides a software platform for digital media measurement and analytics. Their services analyze digital ads to ensure they are viewable, fraud-free, displayed in brand-safe environments, and compliant with advertising standards. They process billions of daily ad impressions across the global web.
How they describe themselves: DoubleVerify is a mission-driven company with a focus on accountability, passion, collaboration, and trailblazing. They aim to make the digital advertising ecosystem stronger and safer. Their engineering organization prioritizes automation and building systems that scale to handle high-throughput media verification workloads.
Size / stage / funding: Estimated 1000-1500 employees; public company (NYSE: DV), highly stable and profitable.
Locations / HQ: Headquartered in New York City, with primary engineering offices in Tel Aviv, London, and additional hubs globally.
Remote policy: Hybrid model is the standard, requiring local office presence for most roles, although some regional remote exceptions exist.
Remote-Canada eligibility: Unclear/Restricted. DoubleVerify has remote postings primarily restricted to the United States (labeled as US Remote) and key office hubs. They do not appear to have an active fully remote hiring setup in Canada for standard engineering roles.
Engineering & tech: High-throughput data processing, real-time analytics, automated data collection, Python, C++, Java, Node.js, and cloud database engines (AWS).
Notable / other: The culture emphasizes high compliance and accountability. They have a public engineering blog sharing high-scale architectural lessons.
