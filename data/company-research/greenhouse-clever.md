# Clever — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:clever | company_type: product

What they do: Clever is a K-12 digital learning platform that provides single sign-on (SSO) and secure rostering infrastructure for schools. Over 111,000 US schools and 95 of the largest 100 US districts use Clever; 60% of American students log in monthly through their platform. The platform handles automated account provisioning from student information systems (SIS) to third-party edtech apps, giving students and teachers a unified digital learning portal.

How they describe themselves: "Software has the potential to empower teachers and create new ways to learn. But one thing must happen first. Everyone must be able to log in—quickly and securely." They position themselves as critical infrastructure for US K-12 edtech. Values emphasize access, security, and student outcomes.

Size / stage / funding: Acquired by Kahoot! in September 2021 for up to $500M. No longer an independent venture-backed company. Now operates as a subsidiary of Kahoot! (Norwegian edtech, Oslo-listed). ~344 employees as of April 2026.

Locations / HQ: HQ in San Francisco, CA; office in Durham, NC. Remote-friendly within continental US only.

Remote policy: "Any role at Clever can be remote within the continental U.S." — explicitly limits remote work to continental US only. Compensation is localized by US region.

Remote-Canada eligibility: No. Explicitly "continental U.S." only per their published remote work policy. Canada is not an eligible location.

Engineering & tech: Platform-focused engineering; roles include Senior Software Engineer (Infrastructure) and School Integrations engineer. Stack appears web-focused given the SSO/rostering product. Infrastructure role would involve platform/reliability work for a large-scale identity/rostering system.

Notable / other: Launched Clever Complete in April 2025 — a single integration for roster, SSO, and LMS support. One of the most widely deployed edtech infrastructure platforms in the US. Parent company Kahoot! is publicly traded in Norway.

Open relevant roles (sample): Senior Software Engineer, Infrastructure (Remote, continental US only); Software Engineer (Mid or Senior), School Integrations (Remote, continental US only)
