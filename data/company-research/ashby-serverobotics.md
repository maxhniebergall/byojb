# Serve Robotics — research
Provider/key: ashby:serverobotics | company_type: product

What they do: Serve Robotics builds autonomous sidewalk delivery robots for last-mile delivery. Their robots operate at walking speed on public sidewalks, delivering food and packages primarily for Uber Eats and similar platforms. The company expanded its scope in 2026 by acquiring Diligent Robotics, adding indoor hospital service robots to its portfolio.

How they describe themselves: "Serve is transforming the delivery industry with our fleet of autonomous delivery robots designed to make delivery more affordable, sustainable, and convenient." The company was founded by "serial entrepreneurs and veteran leaders in robotics and artificial intelligence." Their mission centers on autonomous delivery at scale—"autonomous robotics at scale"—with emphasis on sustainability and last-mile efficiency.

Size / stage / funding: Public company (Nasdaq: SERV), Uber spinoff (2021). Q1 2026 revenue of $3.0M (up 238% sequentially, 578% YoY). Reaffirmed 2026 guidance of ~$26M revenue. Liquidity of $197.4M as of Q1 2026. Expanding to 44 cities in 14 states. Acquired Diligent Robotics in 2026.

Locations / HQ: Headquartered in Redwood City, California. Operations across 44 US cities. Vancouver, BC listed as a location on some job postings (possibly hybrid/office option).

Roles span ML Infrastructure, Software Engineering (ML), Autonomy/Robotics, Operations Tools.
