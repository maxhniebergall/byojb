# Featherless AI — research
Provider/key: ashby:featherlessai | company_type: product

What they do: Featherless AI is a serverless LLM inference hosting platform providing unified API access to 30,000+ open-source AI models (language, vision, audio). They handle GPU orchestration, model load-balancing, and scaling so customers don't need to manage infrastructure. Flat-rate subscription pricing ($10–$200/month), unlimited tokens, no per-token billing surprises. Customers include Ubisoft, Dropbox, Cisco, Meta, and Hugging Face.

How they describe themselves: "One API key. Instant access. Freedom to reliably deploy any open model effortlessly." Core mission framed as "The future of AI is open." Built by researchers contributing to RWKV (a Linux Foundation project). They emphasize openness over proprietary vendor lock-in, privacy ("no prompt logging"), and accessibility. Also operates OpenClaw, an AI agent platform.

Size / stage / funding: ~30 employees (up from 17 in 2025). Founded 2024. Series A: $20M (April 2026), co-led by AMD Ventures and Airbus Ventures; additional backers include BMW i Ventures, Kickstart Ventures, Panache Ventures, Wavemaker Ventures. Prior seed: $5M (2025, Airbus Ventures). ARR ~$4M as of 2026.
