# CircleCI — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:circleci | company_type: product

What they do: CircleCI is a continuous integration and continuous delivery (CI/CD) platform — "the world's largest shared CI/CD platform." It automates the build, test, and deployment pipeline for software teams. The platform integrates with GitHub, Bitbucket, and other VCS tools. Offers cloud-hosted and self-hosted (server) versions. Founded 2011, San Francisco.

How they describe themselves: Core values: "Understand the problem — Seek clarity and write down problems before solving." "Think like the customer — Prioritize customer needs and perspectives." "Own the outcome — Connect success to individual roles using data-driven thinking." "Learn fast — Experiment, accept failure, and prioritize learning." "Do our best work — Embrace diverse perspectives." Philosophy: "foster connection, collaboration, and productivity to help everyone do their best work, in a hybrid model." Described as distributed-first: "With team members across the United States, Canada, United Kingdom, Ireland, Germany, Mexico, and Japan." Async communication is treated as "essential infrastructure."

Size / stage / funding: Series F (2021, $100M at $1.7B valuation). Total funding ~$316M. ~359 employees. Revenue ~$55.7M in 2024. No new major funding since 2021. Privately held. Named to Forbes' America's Best Startup Employers. Founded 2011 — mature company for a startup. 3 acquisitions (Vamp.io, Ponicode Systems, and one other).

Locations / HQ: San Francisco HQ. Team members in US, Canada, UK, Ireland, Germany, Mexico, Japan. Multiple Canada job postings confirmed: Software Engineer (Pipelines) Toronto $113K-141K CAD, Software Engineer (Web Experiences) Toronto hybrid $124K-155K CAD, remote Senior Software Engineer Canada. Support Engineer fully remote Canada/US.

Remote policy: Hybrid model — office or remote. Distributed since inception. Strong async culture with documented practices: async communication in shared channels, recorded meetings, visible calendars, periodic in-person "Small Hands" team events. "The best methods for working on a distributed team will vary by the team." Fully remote support for some roles; hybrid Toronto for others.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Confirmed yes — Canada explicitly listed as a hiring region. Multiple CAD-salary postings in Toronto and remote-Canada confirmed. Fully remote Canada roles exist alongside Toronto-hybrid roles. Strong match.

Engineering & tech: CI/CD pipeline infrastructure, build and test automation, cloud orchestration, container/Docker orchestration, Kubernetes, workflow engine, Clojure (pipeline role confirmed), distributed systems. The core product is developer tooling infrastructure. "Engineers that really care about what they are building, how they are building, and its impact."

Notable / other: Oldest company in this batch (founded 2011) — stable, established product with proven revenue ($55.7M ARR). Slower growth than hypergrowth startups. No major funding since 2021 suggesting profitability path or capital efficiency. Acquired Ponicode (AI unit testing, 2022) and Vamp.io (progressive delivery). Strong remote culture with documented async practices — good fit for async, low-chaos preference. Canada presence is well-established.

Open relevant roles (sample): Senior Software Engineer, Software Engineer
