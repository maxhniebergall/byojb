# Verantos — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:verantos | company_type: product

What they do: Verantos generates high-validity real-world evidence (RWE) for life sciences organizations. They de-identify, enrich, and integrate data from electronic health records, medical claims, and registries to produce evidence usable for regulatory submissions, reimbursement decisions, and clinical research. Their Verantos Evidence Platform is built on AWS, integrates with all major cloud providers and analytic environments, and is HITRUST-CSF certified. Clients include pharmaceutical firms, academic medical centers, and government agencies (FDA, NIH, NSF).

How they describe themselves: "No other company provides RWD of sufficient quality to generate high-validity RWE at scale." Positioned as the only RWE firm with peer-reviewed science validated and funded by NIH, NSF, and FDA. Bootstrapped, employee-owned, and profitable — disciplined approach to long-term value creation. Founded by Dan Riskin (CEO, Clinical Professor at Stanford, expert in healthcare AI, serial entrepreneur). Based in Silicon Valley.

Size / stage / funding: ~57–76 employees (2024–2026 sources). Revenue ~$15.8M (2024). Bootstrapped / minimal outside funding — first funding round 2018, latest a Grant round (February 2025). Profitable and employee-owned.

Locations / HQ: Menlo Park, California (HQ / Silicon Valley). Clinical Annotator role in Pune, India. All engineering roles are remote US.

Remote policy: Remote-friendly. Engineering and clinical roles are remote-eligible but US-based only.

Remote-Canada eligibility: No — confirmed. All open remote roles explicitly state "Remote (U.S. based)" or "United States only." The Himalayas listing for Verantos notes employees exclusively in the United States. The Senior Platform Engineer ($180K–$220K) and Senior Data Engineer roles are both US-only.

Engineering & tech: The Verantos Evidence Platform is built on AWS with Snowflake, GitHub Actions, Terraform, and containerized services. Senior Platform Engineer responsibilities: CI/CD pipeline maintenance for a multi-project monorepo (predictable, fast, team-operable), infrastructure ownership, developer experience, cross-team communication and buy-in. Senior Data Engineer role involves integrating heterogeneous real-world data sources (EHR, claims, registries) using data science and AI approaches. Stack is AWS + Snowflake + Terraform + GitHub Actions — clean modern tooling.

Notable / other: Verantos is an unusually small (~57–76 employees), bootstrapped, profitable healthcare data company. Employee ownership model suggests stable, low-chaos culture with long-term orientation. The platform engineering and data engineering roles are technically clean and meaningful. However, US-only hiring is a hard constraint. The company's niche (RWE for pharma/FDA) is specialized but stable.

Open relevant roles (sample): Senior Data Engineer (Remote, U.S. based); Senior Platform Engineer (Remote, U.S. based, $180K–$220K)
