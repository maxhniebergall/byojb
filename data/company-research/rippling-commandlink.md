# Commandlink — research
Provider/key: rippling:commandlink | company_type: product

What they do: CommandLink is an enterprise IT operations SaaS platform that unifies network, security, and communications infrastructure — including SD-WAN, SASE, UCaaS, CCaaS, managed security, monitoring, ticketing, and support — into a single pane of glass. They help corporations consolidate vendor sprawl and resolve IT incidents faster. They serve SMB through enterprise customers and operate a 24/7 global NOC/SOC.

How they describe themselves: "The Fastest Way to Fix IT Vendor Sprawl & Optimize Incident Resolution." Their remote-first culture page states: "our remote-first culture ensures you have the freedom to produce your best work, no matter where you are on the globe." They emphasize diversity, employee ownership, project autonomy, and collaborative culture. Core values include inclusion, ownership, and growth.

Size / stage / funding: ~299 employees as of April 2026 (grew 46% YoY). Annual revenue of $31.6M. Bootstrapped — no outside funding raised. Private company headquartered in Everett, Washington.

Locations / HQ: Everett, Washington (HQ). Remote-first — operates without geographic restrictions for most roles.

Remote policy: Explicitly remote-first with global hiring. The careers page states employees can work "from anywhere globally without commute requirements." However, open Platform Engineering (Go) roles are listed as "Argentina; Brazil" — not US or Canada.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Unclear/No — despite the remote-first claim, current Platform Engineering and Software Engineer (Go) job postings are explicitly located in Argentina/Brazil. No Canada-specific or US-based engineering openings visible in current listings. The Channel Director role lists Vancouver, Canada as an option.

Engineering & tech: Platform built on ITSM, cloud networking, SD-WAN, UCaaS, managed security, bandwidth solutions, and analytics. Platform Engineering roles use Go. Engineering involves operating Kubernetes clusters, Kafka, OpenSearch, and cloud environments. Modernizing legacy PHP into service-oriented architecture.

Notable / other: The only Canada-flagged role was a Channel Director (sales) with Vancouver option. All current technical/engineering roles are LATAM-based. Despite remote-first branding, current technical hiring is geographically concentrated in South America.

Open relevant roles (sample): Senior Software Engineer, Platform Engineering (Go) — Argentina/Brazil; Software Engineer, Go — Argentina/Brazil
