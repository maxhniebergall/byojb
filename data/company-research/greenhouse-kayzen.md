# Kayzen — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:kayzen | company_type: product

What they do: Kayzen is a mobile demand-side platform (DSP) for programmatic advertising. They enable advertisers, app developers, agencies, and media buyers to run programmatic customer acquisition, retargeting, and brand performance campaigns with full transparency and control over data. The platform processes 160B+ daily ad requests with sub-millisecond latency requirements across global data centers. Describes itself as "democratizing programmatic advertising" by offering self-serve and managed service options with a transparency-first philosophy.

How they describe themselves: "Democratize programmatic advertising" — giving players of all sizes access to enterprise-grade mobile programmatic tools previously only available to large holding companies. Founded by three veterans with "nearly 40 years of combined experience" (Tim Koschella CEO, Puneet Gupta CPO, Servesh Jain CTO). Culture values transparency, performance, and control. 70+ employees ("Kayzenians") across 10+ global offices.

Size / stage / funding: Seed-stage company. Raised $4M total (Seed VC round, 2020). Investors: InReach Ventures, Lifeline Ventures. Revenue approximately $15.7M ARR as of 2023. ~70-115 employees. Founded 2019, HQ Berlin, Germany.

Locations / HQ: Headquartered in Berlin, Germany. 10+ global offices including London, New York, Bangalore. Predominantly EMEA/India-focused team.

Remote policy: Remote positions available but explicitly scoped to EMEA and India regions. DevOps Engineer role listed as "Bangalore, Remote India." LLM Platform Engineer listed as "Remote EMEA, Remote India." No North American remote listed for engineering roles.

Remote-Canada eligibility: No — engineering roles explicitly scoped to EMEA/India only. Canada is not an eligible location for any observed engineering role.

Engineering & tech: Platform Engineering team manages RTB (real-time bidding) infrastructure at ~2M requests/second. DevOps role involves bare-metal server provisioning, IaC (Terraform/Ansible/Puppet/Chef), load balancing (HAProxy/Nginx), monitoring (Prometheus, Grafana, InfluxDB), CI/CD (Jenkins/Airflow), Kubernetes. LLM Platform role involves LLM API integration, RAG pipelines, vector databases, internal AI SDKs/frameworks — focused on developer productivity tools not model training.

Notable / other: Seed-stage (only $4M raised) but generating ~$15M ARR — lean bootstrapped-style operation. Technically interesting real-time bidding infrastructure. All engineering hiring focused on EMEA/Bangalore teams. The LLM Platform role is AI tooling (internal developer tooling), not consumer AI.

Open relevant roles (sample): DevOps Engineer (Remote India); LLM Platform Engineer/Lead (Remote EMEA, Remote India)
