What they do: Pathway is a real-time data processing and AI framework company. Their core product is a live data framework ("Pathway") that unifies batch and streaming data pipelines for real-time machine learning and AI applications. It benchmarks at ~90x faster than existing streaming solutions and uniquely supports "unlearning" (removing data from trained models). They also build post-transformer frontier AI models capable of continual learning and reasoning.
