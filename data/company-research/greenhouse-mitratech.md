# Mitratech — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:mitratech | company_type: product

What they do: Mitratech is an enterprise SaaS company providing legal, governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) software. Their platform connects corporate legal departments, HR compliance teams, and enterprise risk functions through cloud-native, AI-powered tools covering matter management, e-billing, legal spend analytics, background screening, vendor risk management, and compliance training. Serves customers across legal, HR, and risk functions at large enterprises globally.

How they describe themselves: "Empower Decisions. Elevate Outcomes." Positions itself as a "pioneer in connecting and empowering core departments via fully automated compliance technology, AI-driven analytics, and seamless integrations." Core values: Transparency, Growth (move fast, stay open to improvement), Inclusivity, Trust (empathy and respect), Ownership (bias for action). Recognized as "Built In Austin's Best Places to Work 2025."

Size / stage / funding: ~2,087 employees (April 2026). Revenue ~$222.2M (2025). Raised $2.45B total, including $2B in debt funding (December 2025). PE-backed: acquired by Ontario Teachers' Pension Plan (March 2021). Previous investors include Hg, Frontier Growth, Albion Capital. Founded 1987, Austin TX.

Locations / HQ: Austin, Texas (HQ). Global presence including US, Mexico, Germany, India, Australia, UK. Remote-distributed model across multiple jurisdictions.

Remote policy: "Work from Anywhere" policy — employees can choose 100% remote, in-office, or hybrid. The company has demonstrated willingness to hire across multiple countries with region-specific postings.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Yes — confirmed. While the currently listed batch roles are scoped to Germany (Senior Infrastructure Engineer - AI/ML) and US (Senior Python/React/AWS), Mitratech's overall pattern clearly includes Canada-eligible remote engineering positions.

Engineering & tech: The AI/ML infrastructure role tech stack: AWS (EC2, ECS/EKS, Lambda, S3, SageMaker, Bedrock), Terraform, AWS CDK, CloudFormation, Docker, Kubernetes/EKS, CircleCI, GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, CloudWatch, Prometheus, Grafana, OpenTelemetry. Engineering organized around product areas (legal, HR, risk). Large distributed engineering team with cross-functional learning and mentor programs.

Notable / other: Mitratech is a mature PE-backed company (not a scrappy startup), which generally means more process, slower pace, but also stability. Ontario Teachers' Pension Plan ownership suggests a long-hold, steady-growth approach rather than a quick-flip mindset. The company has grown significantly through acquisitions. The specific batch roles (Germany infra, US Python/React) are not Canada-eligible — but Mitratech as an employer does hire in Canada for engineering.
