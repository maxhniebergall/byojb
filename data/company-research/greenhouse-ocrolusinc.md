# Ocrolus Inc. — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:ocrolusinc | company_type: product

What they do: Ocrolus is an AI workflow and analytics platform for lenders. The platform automates financial document analysis (bank statements, pay stubs, tax documents) for underwriting decisions in small business lending, mortgage, auto finance, and consumer lending. Combines AI extraction with a human-in-the-loop verification layer to achieve 99%+ accuracy. Processes nearly one million credit applications monthly. Serves 400+ customers including Better Mortgage, Brex, Enova, LendingClub, PayPal, Plaid, SoFi, and Square.

How they describe themselves: "AI workflow and analytics platform for lenders" — describes itself as helping lenders "make faster and more accurate financial decisions." Founded 2014 by Sam Bobley. Describes itself as a "remote-first company" committed to employee growth and ownership. Engineering culture emphasizes system ownership and meaningful impact.

Size / stage / funding: ~650–833 employees (various sources, 2025–2026). Revenue ~$91.6M ARR. Raised ~$122–274M total across 4 funding rounds. M&A activity noted (April 2025). HQ: New York, NY. Founded 2014.

Locations / HQ: New York, NY (headquarters). Engineering presence in India (Gurugram, Noida — remote and in-office). US remote roles available.

Remote policy: Remote-first company. Engineering roles split between US remote and India remote. The "App Infra" roles specifically are listed as "Remote - India" (not US or Canada).

Remote-Canada eligibility: No — confirmed not Canada-eligible. The Senior Software Engineer - App Infra and Staff Software Engineer - App Infra roles in the batch are both explicitly listed as "Remote - India." US remote engineering roles exist (e.g., Mortgage Forward Deployed Engineer) but are US-only. No Canada-eligible engineering postings found.

Engineering & tech: App Infra team stack: Python, Golang, Flask, PostgreSQL, MySQL, AWS/Azure/GCP, Docker, Kubernetes, microservices architecture. Role involves designing scalable distributed systems, owning microservices, building cloud-based software products, and scaling data pipelines. Emphasis on distributed systems, API development, and automated testing. Senior role is 8–12 years experience with team leadership expected.

Notable / other: Ocrolus had M&A activity in April 2025, which introduces some uncertainty about company direction. The India engineering hub is the center of gravity for technical roles. Despite calling itself "remote-first," the infra roles are firmly India-scoped. The platform is embedded in US financial infrastructure, which limits geographic flexibility for most roles.

Open relevant roles (sample): Senior Software Engineer - App Infra (Remote, India); Staff Software Engineer - App Infra (Remote, India)
