# Zoom — research
Provider/key: workday:zoom/zoom | company_type: product

What they do: Zoom is a global unified communications and collaboration platform providing video conferencing, voice, webinars, contact center, and increasingly AI-powered communication tools. Their infrastructure underpins real-time communications for hundreds of millions of users worldwide. The company has expanded into "Agentic AI" products for enterprise workflows.

How they describe themselves: "Join us in connecting the world and changing the way people work." Core value system built on "Care" — for customers, teammates, the company, communities, and personal wellness. Foundational belief: "in order to deliver happiness to our customers, our employees should be happy at work." The company describes a "structured hybrid working environment."

Size / stage / funding: Public company (NASDAQ: ZM). ~8,400 employees. Revenue ~$4.7B (FY2025). Post-pandemic normalization underway; investing heavily in AI integration (Zoom AI Companion, Agentic AI platform). Established large-cap technology company.

Locations / HQ: San Jose, California (HQ). Global offices: US (multiple), UK, India, Australia, Japan, Singapore, Germany. Canada is NOT listed as a primary office location.

Remote policy: "Structured hybrid working environment." Role types designated as Hybrid, Remote, or In-Person per posting. Current DevOps/infrastructure job postings (Staff DevOps Engineer, Senior DevOps Engineer, Infrastructure Engineer) are all listed as San Jose, California, on-site — not remote. Remote roles found only for India or specific US-based positions.

Remote-Canada eligibility: No — Canada is not listed as a hiring location. Current infrastructure, DevOps, and engineering roles are either San Jose on-site or US-remote only. "Immigration sponsorship is not available" noted on postings. No Canadian positions identified in current job board listings.

Engineering & tech: Infrastructure team manages global communications infrastructure at scale — video, audio, real-time streaming, datacenters. DevOps team focuses on reliability, scalability, CI/CD, disaster recovery, zero-downtime deployments. The Zoom Gateway DevOps team works on ZoomAI and ML service infrastructure. Stack: Linux, cloud (AWS/Azure/GCP), CI/CD pipelines, Kubernetes assumed at scale.

Notable / other: Zoom went through significant post-COVID normalization including layoffs in 2023. The company is refocusing on enterprise and AI features. Despite being a remote-work icon, their own engineering hiring is heavily San Jose-centric. Agentic AI push may create new platform/infra roles but current pipeline shows no Canada eligibility.

Open relevant roles (sample): Staff DevOps Engineer (San Jose, CA — on-site), Senior DevOps Engineer (San Jose, CA — on-site), Infrastructure Engineer (San Jose, CA), Software Engineer roles (US-based)
