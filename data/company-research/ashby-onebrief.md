# Onebrief — research
Provider/key: ashby:onebrief | company_type: product

What they do: Onebrief builds collaborative software for military headquarters and command operations. Their platform is designed as "the operating system for modern command" — providing agentic workflows, decision intelligence, and collaborative planning tools for military staffs. The platform is deployed on classified military networks (SIPR and JWICS) and is integral to three of the four largest global operational plans (OPLANs). Founded 2019 by Grant Demaree (former US Army officer), Y Combinator Summer 2021 batch.

How they describe themselves: "Fuses agentic workflows, decision intelligence, and collaborative tools into a unified, resilient platform to transform how commands operate and make decisions." Core values: (1) Ownership & Excellence — "own the consequences of our work"; (2) Truth-Seeking — "high-trust environment," first-principles problem-solving; (3) User-Centric Mission Focus — "love the user, respect our adversaries, understand the game"; (4) Clear Communication & Strategy. Describes itself as "mission-driven" and "fully remote organization."

Size / stage / funding: Raised $53M total; reached $650M valuation in January 2025. Team grew 2.6x in the last year and 5.1x in the last two years — 100+ employees worldwide. Fast-growing defense-tech startup.

Locations / HQ: Honolulu, Hawaii, USA. Fully remote organization across 33 US states. Employees also work alongside customers at military commands worldwide.

Remote policy: "Fully remote organization." Team distributed across 33 US states. Annual in-person gathering in October plus team meetups.

Remote-Canada eligibility: No — US-only remote. The Principal Infrastructure Engineer role requires "willingness to obtain and maintain eligibility for a Top Secret clearance with SCI." Top Secret/SCI clearance eligibility effectively requires US citizenship (or permanent residency at minimum), making the role inaccessible to Canadian candidates. All role listings found specify "United States, Remote."

Engineering & tech: Infrastructure/platform roles focus on: Kubernetes, Terraform/Ansible, AWS + on-premises environments, air-gapped/classified network deployments, Python, Bash, IaC. Principal Infrastructure Engineer requires 10+ years infrastructure + 5+ years Platform Engineering/DevOps/SRE. Compensation $220K-$270K base + equity.

Notable / other: One of the more technically interesting defense-tech infrastructure roles — classified/air-gapped deployments, real operational criticality. However, defense contracts (SBIR III) and military clearance requirements make this essentially inaccessible to non-US citizens. $650M valuation at 100+ employees is a very high multiple — high growth phase.

Open relevant roles (sample): Staff Infrastructure Engineer; Principal Infrastructure Engineer (requires Top Secret/SCI clearance eligibility)
