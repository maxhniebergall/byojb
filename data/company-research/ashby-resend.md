# Resend — research
Provider/key: ashby:resend | company_type: product

What they do: Resend is an email infrastructure platform for developers. They provide an Email API, SMTP service, inbound email, automations, and templates — positioned as "the Stripe of email" for developer teams. Core product: reliable email delivery with excellent developer experience (SDKs, React Email component library). Focus on transactional and product email (not marketing/ESP). Acquired Mergent (Apr 2025, task scheduling) and Briefer (Aug 2025, data notebooks), broadening the platform beyond email.

How they describe themselves: "Building the future of email." Mission: fix email for developers — solve deliverability, modernize DX, bring the same quality bar to email that Stripe brought to payments. Values: quality ("people who care deeply about quality"), developer-centricity, "10x innovation," extremely user-centric. Culture: small, tight-knit, fully remote global team. Founders: Bu Kinoshita and Zeno Rocha (Brazilian founders, Y Combinator W23).

Size / stage / funding: ~28 employees (Dec 2025, including recent acquisitions). Founded Aug 2022 (public launch). Y Combinator W23. Series A: $18M (Dec 2024) from a16z and angel investors (Dylan Field/Figma, Guillermo Rauch/Vercel). Previously: $3M seed (Apr 2023). Total raised: ~$21M. HQ: San Francisco, CA (nominal), but fully remote global team.

Locations / HQ: Nominal HQ in San Francisco, CA. Fully distributed team — team offsites in Lisbon (2023), Brazil (2024), Tuscany (2025). No fixed office requirement.

Remote policy: Fully remote. Global team ("Americas," "Europe," and "United States" regions mentioned in job listings). Unlimited PTO. Work-from-home culture embedded from founding. Offsites 1–2x/year for in-person connection.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Likely yes but not explicitly confirmed. A previous Backend Engineer (Back-end) listing on BuiltIn stated candidates must "consistently work in Americas timezone" — Canada Mountain Time qualifies. The careers page lists roles as "Americas / Remote" and "United States / Remote." Canada is in Americas timezone. One confirmed archived job listing mentioned Canada as eligible. No explicit Canada exclusion found. The small team size and global remote culture suggest Canadian candidates can apply to Americas-region roles.

Engineering & tech: Email delivery infrastructure — MTA (Mail Transfer Agent), SMTP protocol, DNS/deliverability (DKIM, SPF, DMARC), queuing, retry logic, rate limiting, ISP feedback loops. "Core Sending" team is the infrastructure heart of the platform. Stack: Node.js/TypeScript primary (Vercel/Next.js ecosystem influence), PostgreSQL, Redis. Cloud: AWS inferred. Recent acquisitions added task scheduling (Mergent) and data tooling (Briefer). Infrastructure backend roles focus on MTA engineering and sending infrastructure reliability.

Notable / other: YC-backed, a16z-backed — strong investor pedigree for a ~28 person team. Resend is widely used in the developer community; React Email (open source by Resend) has strong GitHub presence. The product is infrastructure plumbing (email delivery) not AI hype. Acquisitions of Mergent and Briefer in 2025 show ambition to expand beyond email into a developer platform. Very small team (28 people) means high individual ownership and low bureaucracy — could also mean less stability/structure.

Open relevant roles (sample): Backend Engineer (Infra), Core Sending; Backend Engineer (MTA), Core Sending.
