# Datadog — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:datadog | company_type: product

What they do: Datadog is the leading cloud observability and security platform. They provide infrastructure monitoring, APM (application performance monitoring), log management, network monitoring, security (SIEM/CSPM/CWPP), CI/CD visibility, database monitoring, and AI observability (Bits AI). Their platform is a "single pane of glass" for engineering and DevOps/SRE teams running cloud infrastructure. Collects tens of trillions of data events per day, running on millions of hosts globally.

How they describe themselves: Mission: "to build the best monitoring and security platform for developers, IT operations teams and business users in the cloud age" with a vision of being "the single pane of glass for modern engineering teams." Core values: transparency, collaboration, customer focus, continuous learning, ownership, and innovation. Culture: highly engineering-driven, strong bias toward shipping fast and iterating, high ownership expected. "Everyone is always enthusiastic about sharing what they are working on and we are all learning something new on a daily basis." Feedback culture focused on growth, not blame.

Size / stage / funding: ~8,100+ employees globally. Public company (NASDAQ: DDOG) since 2019. Market cap fluctuates ($20B–$50B range). Revenue run rate ~$2.5B+ (2024/25). Profitable. Very established public company.

Locations / HQ: Headquarters in New York City. Offices in: New York, San Francisco, Boston, Dublin, Paris, Tokyo, Sydney, Toronto, and others. Americas, APAC, EMEA regions. Remote positions also available.

Remote policy: Flexible work culture — "supports remote, hybrid, and office cultures." Dedicated remote positions section on careers site. Provides Wi-Fi and home office reimbursement for remote workers. Not fully remote-first; many roles require office presence. The company has coworking spaces throughout the USA, Canada, Mexico, and South America.

Remote-Canada eligibility: YES — confirmed. Datadog has a Toronto office and actively hires remote engineers in Canada. Indeed Canada shows ~80 Datadog remote openings. Himalayas.app, Working Nomads, and other aggregators confirm active Canada-remote posting history. The company explicitly lists Canada in its Americas hiring region and provides coworking stipends in Canada.

Engineering & tech: Go, Python, Java, C++, React. Systems run on Kubernetes, multi-region, multi-cloud. Backend engineers work on large-scale distributed data pipelines (ingest, query, analytics), the Lakehouse (analytics data platform), AI/LLM integrations (Bits AI), product analytics, and SRE/reliability tooling. Engineering teams organized by product domain; the Lakehouse/Data Platform and Backend teams are directly relevant.

Notable / other: One of the most technically rigorous observability companies in the industry. Acquired several companies (Sqreen, Hdiv, Ozcode, etc.). Strong SRE and distributed systems culture. Senior engineers work at massive scale. Competitive salaries. Work/life balance reviews are mixed (some teams intense, others reasonable). 

Open relevant roles (sample): Senior Backend Software Engineer - Product Analytics, Senior Software Engineer - Analytics Data Platform Lakehouse, Senior Software Engineer - Backend, Senior Software Engineer - Bits AI SRE
