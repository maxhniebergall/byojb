# Hugging Face — research
Provider/key: workable:huggingface | company_type: product

What they do: Hugging Face is the leading open-source AI/ML platform company. They build and maintain transformative open-source libraries (Transformers, Datasets, Accelerate, PEFT, Diffusers, Tokenizers), host the Hub (a model/dataset/space repository with 500k+ models), and provide cloud-hosted inference and training services. Their mission is to "democratize good machine learning." Core customers are AI practitioners, researchers, and enterprises building on ML.

How they describe themselves: Mission is "democratizing machine learning." They describe themselves as the "GitHub of machine learning." Culture emphasis: async-first, globally distributed, no required hours. They value open collaboration with the broader research community and the open-source ecosystem. Tone is highly technical, research-oriented, collaborative, and mission-driven.

Size / stage / funding: ~700 employees in 20+ countries. Raised over $235M; latest known round was a $235M Series D at a $4.5B valuation (2023, led by Salesforce Ventures). Also backed by Google, Amazon, Nvidia, Intel, IBM, Qualcomm, and a16z. Not yet public. Profitable on some product lines.

Locations / HQ: Headquarters in New York City and Paris. No required office attendance — fully distributed. Hubs in NYC and Paris but team globally distributed.

Remote policy: Remote is the default. Employees work "whenever works best for them" with no required online hours (async-first). The company explicitly hires from 20+ countries globally. Remote Canada roles exist; their open roles on Workable include "EMEA Remote" and "US Remote" designations. "International Remote" roles have also been posted.

Remote-Canada eligibility: UNCLEAR — partially confirmed. The company is globally distributed and hires from 20+ countries. Current open titles listed include "US Remote" and "EMEA Remote" variants, with no explicit "Canada Remote" designation in the listed titles. However, previous postings have included "International Remote" variants that would include Canada. Compensation is geo-tiered. Canada may fall under a non-EMEA/non-US tier or could be lumped with "International Remote." Direct verification via application or recruiter contact recommended.

Engineering & tech: Python-first. Core libraries: Transformers (PyTorch/JAX/TensorFlow), Datasets, Accelerate, Diffusers, PEFT. Hub infrastructure: Rust + Python backend, Git LFS-based storage. Open-source contributor culture — engineers spend significant time engaging with the community, reviewing PRs, writing blog posts. Not a traditional infra/platform shop; emphasis is on ML frameworks and the open-source ecosystem.

Notable / other: One of the most influential companies in the AI/open-source space (2023–2026). Major industry voice on open vs. closed AI. Strong research partnerships. Geo-tiered pay model means Canada-based employees may earn below US rates.
