Provider/key: ashby:1password | company_type: product

What they do: 1Password is a password manager and identity security platform for individuals, families, and enterprises. They help organizations store, share, and autofill credentials, manage secrets, and enforce access policies. Their product spans consumer (1Password Families) and B2B (1Password Business/Teams) segments, with growing AI and developer tooling integrations.

How they describe themselves: "Pioneers, engineers, and builders, working to secure the digital world." Mission framed around eliminating the security vs. convenience trade-off. Values: impact and belonging ("supported to be bold, learn, grow, and have meaningful impact"), inclusion at the heart of how they work, continuous learning and AI fluency, and community engagement (140+ causes supported through employee-nominated grants and 1Password for Good). Remote-first philosophy described as "intentional, in-person collaboration" — teams come together at key moments (annual offsites, team meetings, customer events) rather than fixed office schedules.
