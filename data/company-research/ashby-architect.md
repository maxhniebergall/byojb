# Architect Labs — research
Provider/key: ashby:architect | company_type: product
What they do: Architect Labs is a developer of an AI-powered system designed to automate and accelerate custom silicon chip design and verification. They aim to make specialized hardware more accessible to AI labs, robotics, and other companies by removing the traditional barriers and resources needed for chip development.
How they describe themselves: They describe themselves as building the "designless semiconductor" industry, automating end-to-end design. They emphasize deep technical exploration across machine learning, design verification, formal methods, and systems software.
Size / stage / funding: Raised $24 million seed funding in June 2026, led by Kindred Ventures, with participation from TQ Ventures, Race Capital, Together Fund, and angels from Google DeepMind, OpenAI, and NVIDIA.
Locations / HQ: Palo Alto, California (HQ) and Bangalore, India.
