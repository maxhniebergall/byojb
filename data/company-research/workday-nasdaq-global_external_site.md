# Nasdaq — research
Provider/key: workday:nasdaq/global_external_site | company_type: product

What they do: Nasdaq is a global technology company that powers capital markets infrastructure, financial services software, and market data. Beyond operating the Nasdaq stock exchange, its technology division delivers platforms for market surveillance, trading technology, financial crime management (Verafin), risk and compliance, and corporate governance software. Their technology serves exchanges, banks, brokers, investment managers, and regulators across 130+ countries.

How they describe themselves: "We power stronger economies, create more equitable opportunities and contribute to a more sustainable world." Mission centered on improving "the integrity, transparency and liquidity of the global economy." Their hybrid work model is called "NasdaqBlend" — "gives employees flexibility to work from home, in the office, or a mix of both." Self-described as "a global team of over 8,500 innovators across 38 countries."

Size / stage / funding: Public company (NASDAQ: NDAQ). ~8,500+ employees globally. Financial Technology division generated 23% ARR growth in 2024 with 114% net revenue retention. Multi-billion dollar revenue. Significant operations (200+ employees) in Canada, US, Sweden, Lithuania, India, Philippines, Australia, UK.

Locations / HQ: Global HQ in New York. Major Canada presence: Toronto (via Nasdaq Verafin, Nasdaq Boardvantage, and exchange technology teams); St. John's, NL (Verafin HQ). 187+ jobs in Greater Toronto Area.

Remote policy: NasdaqBlend hybrid model. Nasdaq Verafin specifically: employees near Toronto or St. John's offices work in-office 3 days/week; remote employees hired with flexibility. Some roles posted as hybrid Toronto. Backend developer roles in Toronto are hybrid on-site. No fully remote (anywhere-in-Canada) roles confirmed for engineering.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Yes, with caveats — Canada is a major Nasdaq location (Toronto, St. John's). Backend Java, Platform Engineering, DevOps Cloud Engineer, and Software Engineer roles are posted for Toronto. These appear to be hybrid (in-office 3 days/week near Toronto), not fully remote. Candidate in Kimberley BC (Mountain Time) would need the role to be remote-Canada, which is not confirmed for engineering roles.

Engineering & tech: Java backend heavy (financial securities industry); Linux environments; full-stack web; Go backend roles at Verafin; Python. Engineering organized around financial market infrastructure, trading platforms, surveillance, AML/fraud (Verafin). Verafin is building agentic AI for AML workflows. Platform Operations and DevOps Cloud Engineer roles present in Toronto.

Notable / other: Verafin (acquired 2021) is Nasdaq's fintech arm focused on financial crime management with $400M+ ARR. Verafin operates Canada-based engineering teams (St. John's, Toronto). Nasdaq recently launched agentic AI workers for AML compliance. Strong, stable large-company employer with significant fintech engineering depth.
