# Unstructured Technologies Inc. — research
Provider/key: ashby:unstructured | company_type: product

What they do: Unstructured (unstructured.io) builds data transformation infrastructure for the AI era. Their platform ingests and transforms unstructured data (PDFs, HTML, images, video, audio, documents) into LLM-ready formats. Their open-source toolkit has been downloaded 52M+ times and is used by 66,000+ companies including ~half of the Fortune 500. They also have a Public Sector vertical serving US government and military clients, with FedRAMP certification and HIPAA compliance.

How they describe themselves: "Whatever it is, we can structure it." Positioned as the standard for enterprise unstructured data transformation in the LLM age. Strong US government/defense focus on the public sector side (employs veterans, values prior government service). Describes itself as fully remote and values driven engineering culture.
