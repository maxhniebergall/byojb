# Backblaze — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:backblaze | company_type: product
What they do: Backblaze provides cloud storage and backup solutions. They design and operate custom storage software and hardware (famous for their open-source Storage Pod design) to offer low-cost, high-performance object storage (B2 Cloud Storage) and computer backups.
How they describe themselves: Driven by the mission to make storing data "astonishingly easy and low-cost." Their core values are centered on trust, fairness, and transparency, summarized in their simple philosophy: "Be good" and "Build a good product." They are highly open, frequently publishing detailed, industry-standard hard drive reliability data ("Drive Stats").
Size / stage / funding: Public company (NASDAQ: BLZE), established, profitable/sustainable growth, ~300+ employees.
Locations / HQ: San Mateo, California (HQ) with global data centers (US and Europe).
Remote policy: Remote-first, with a highly distributed global workforce.
Remote-Canada eligibility: Verified yes. They employ remote team members in Canada.
Engineering & tech: High-scale data storage systems, storage infrastructure, file backup protocols. Tech stack features Go, C++, Java, Python, and custom platform automation.
Notable / other: Main developer and engineering blog categories include Tech Lab, focusing on how they manage exabytes of data and scale their storage cloud. Very transparent, low-politics culture.
Open relevant roles (sample): Senior Infrastructure Engineer; Storage Platform Software Engineer; Senior Backend Engineer.
