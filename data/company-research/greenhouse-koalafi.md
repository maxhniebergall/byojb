# Koalafi — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:koalafi | company_type: product

What they do: Koalafi is a fintech platform providing lease-to-own and second-look financing solutions for retailers and their customers. Their platform enables over 28,000 merchant partners to offer pay-over-time options to customers across all credit profiles, including non-prime consumers who don't qualify for traditional financing. Loans issued by The Bank of Missouri. Koalafi serves customers directly and powers merchant integrations through a unified financing platform.

How they describe themselves: "Pay over time plans that are simply for everyone." Mission is to "offer lease-to-own financing that drives real growth for your business and your customers." Entrepreneurial spirit, remote associates described as having "regular opportunities to travel to corporate offices" and participate in virtual social events. Culture emphasizes inclusivity and collaboration.

Size / stage / funding: ~266 employees (April 2026). Total funding reported variously as $41M–$195.5M (depending on source). Headquartered in Richmond, Virginia. Founded 2014 (originally West Creek Financial, rebranded to Koalafi).

Locations / HQ: Richmond, Virginia (HQ). Arlington, Virginia (satellite). Remote-distributed across US.

Remote policy: Remote work is available; remote associates are distributed across the US with optional in-office access at Richmond or Arlington. Regular virtual social events mentioned for remote workers.

Remote-Canada eligibility: No — all engineering roles are explicitly "Remote, United States" or specify US locations (Richmond VA, Arlington VA). The Lead Platform Engineer posting states "eligible regions: United States only." No Canada eligibility found.

Engineering & tech: Platform engineering team owns CI/CD, Terraform IaC, Kubernetes production ops, AWS infrastructure, observability (metrics/logging/distributed tracing), and incident response. The Lead Platform Engineer role mentions Istio/service mesh, AWS RDS/Aurora, serverless (Lambda), Python/Go/Bash scripting. AI coding tools (GitHub Copilot, Cursor, Claude) also listed. The "Engineering Lead, Platform Engineering" role is a player-manager hybrid with substantial IC work.

Notable / other: The "Lead Platform Engineer" role is primarily a people-management position (hiring, 1:1s, performance reviews) that carries technical work secondarily. This is a management track, not pure IC. Koalafi operates in the regulated consumer lending space, which implies stability but also compliance overhead.

Open relevant roles (sample): Engineering Lead, Platform Engineering; Lead Platform Engineer
