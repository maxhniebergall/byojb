# Wizard — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:wizardcommerce | company_type: product

What they do: Wizard is an AI-powered shopping agent that searches across retailer websites, customer reviews, editorial content, and social content to return curated product recommendations. The platform integrates with major retailers including Amazon, Best Buy, Walmart, Target, Apple, Costco, and Wayfair. Co-founded by Marc Lore (former Walmart e-commerce chief) and Melissa Bridgeford, the company ran a private beta for several years before emerging from stealth in February 2026.

How they describe themselves: "Our mission is to build a world where finding what you need is simple, fast, and fun—and where scouring the internet is a thing of the past." The company describes itself as having "a small, talented team of builders across AI, product and commerce" with a "digital-first mentality." They emphasize "early stage upside" and hosting "several in-person gatherings" annually for team connection. Tone is startup-enthusiastic and consumer-focused.

Size / stage / funding: Raised $50M Series A in September 2021 led by NEA, with participation from Accel, A-Rod, and Capstar Ventures. Total raised approximately $51.4M. Emerged from stealth February 2026. Small early-stage team; headcount not publicly disclosed.

Locations / HQ: Headquartered in New York, NY. Remote-first across the US.

Remote policy: Fully remote-first. "Our opportunities aren't limited by location" — employees can work from anywhere that allows them to be productive. Company hosts annual in-person gatherings.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Unclear — not stated. All job postings explicitly list "Remote, USA" as the location. No Canadian candidates mentioned. Likely US-only given "Remote, USA" labeling on all roles.

Engineering & tech: Focused on ML/AI engineering — roles include ML Engineer (Relevance & Learning Systems), Senior ML Engineer (Inference Platform), Senior Python Engineer, and AI Applied Scientist. Stack appears Python-heavy with ML/inference focus. Small team building consumer-facing AI.

Notable / other: Pivoted from B2B SMS commerce to consumer-first AI agent during generative AI wave. Partnered with Best Buy for native checkout feature at public launch. Backed by prominent investors. Very early-stage product/team.
