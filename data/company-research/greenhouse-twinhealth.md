# Twin Health — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:twinhealth | company_type: product

What they do: Twin Health is a digital health company using AI to reverse chronic metabolic diseases — type 2 diabetes, obesity, and fatty liver disease. Their core technology is the "Whole Body AI Digital Twin," a patent-pending individualized model of a person's metabolism built from continuous data (CGMs, smartwatches, meal logs). The platform combines AI recommendations with clinical care (health coaches, physicians) to achieve disease reversal. Sells to employers, health plans, and individual members. Over 1 billion people globally affected by their target conditions; 12 papers published in top medical journals.

How they describe themselves: "Reverse chronic diseases and help people live healthier and happier lives." Founded by Jahangir Mohammed (serial entrepreneur, former Jasper/Cisco CEO, 80+ patents). Board includes former Starbucks CEO Kevin Johnson and former Cleveland Clinic CEO. Medical advisory board of globally recognized endocrinologists. Technology co-founder Terry Poon leads platform and algorithm development. Interdisciplinary team of engineers, scientists, and doctors.

Size / stage / funding: ~640–701 employees (Dec 2025–Jan 2026). Raised $272M total over 4 rounds. Latest: $53M Series E (August 2025) led by Maj Invest of Denmark, boosting valuation to ~$950M. Backed by Sequoia, ICONIQ Growth, Temasek, Sofina, Peak XV. HQ: Mountain View, California. Engineering offices in Chennai and Bangalore, India.

Locations / HQ: Mountain View, CA (HQ). Chennai/Bangalore/Coimbatore, India (engineering center). Remote US positions available.

Remote policy: Flexible — employees can work remotely (100% remote US roles available). The company is described as "remote friendly" but specific roles specify remote US or India office locations. Not globally remote.

Remote-Canada eligibility: No — confirmed. All engineering roles are explicitly "Remote, USA" or India-based. The Lead Software Engineer, Senior AI Engineer, and Senior AI Platform Engineer roles all state "Remote, USA" with requirement to be "authorized to work for any employer in the U.S." Benefits are US-centric ($50/month internet reimbursement, US healthcare). No Canadian positions listed or found.

Engineering & tech: Tech stack: Python (primary), LLMs and Generative AI, RAG, production ML deployment, CI/CD and observability tools, Snowflake (data). "Senior AI Platform Engineer" and "Senior AI Engineer" are core AI team roles — end-to-end AI product lifecycle, evaluation frameworks, conversational AI agents, deep learning. Built on IoT data (CGMs, wearables), machine learning, and digital twin algorithms. Java, JavaScript, Kotlin also used per Built In listing.

Notable / other: Twin Health is a late-stage startup (~$950M valuation) with genuine clinical outcomes and a strong scientific foundation. The engineering work is ML/AI-heavy, not traditional infra. The "AI Platform" framing here means ML platform (model deployment, evaluation, inference), not cloud/network infrastructure. US-only hiring for engineering is a hard constraint.

Open relevant roles (sample): Lead Software Engineer (Remote, USA); Senior AI Platform Engineer (Remote, USA); Senior AI Engineer (Remote, USA)
