# JetBrains — research
Provider/key: greenhouse:jetbrains | company_type: product
What they do: JetBrains creates intelligent developer tools, software IDEs (such as IntelliJ IDEA, PyCharm, WebStorm, ReSharper), team collaboration tools (Space, TeamCity), and is the creator of the Kotlin programming language.
How they describe themselves: Driven by the mission to make professional software development "a more productive and enjoyable experience" by building the strongest, most effective developer tools on earth. They pride themselves on being organically grown, independent, and privately owned, allowing them to focus on product quality rather than external investor pressures.
Size / stage / funding: Established, private company, organic growth/sustainable, ~1,500+ employees.
Locations / HQ: Headquartered in Prague, Czech Republic, with major office hubs across Germany, Netherlands, USA, Poland, Cyprus, and Armenia.
Remote policy: Hybrid or office-bound with remote allowances restricted strictly to regions containing active entities.
Remote-Canada eligibility: No. JetBrains does not have an active employment entity or hiring presence for remote workers located in Canada. Most remote roles are restricted to specific European countries or the United States.
Engineering & tech: High focus on compiler design, static analysis, IDE integration, JVM performance, MLOps, and developer tool ecosystems. Tech stack is heavily Java, Kotlin, C++, Rust, and Go.
Notable / other: They maintain "The Drive to Develop" blog (blog.jetbrains.com), deep-diving into programming paradigms, IDE features, and static code analysis research.
Open relevant roles (sample): Senior Kotlin Developer; MLOps Infrastructure Engineer; IDE Platform Developer.
Sources: jetbrains.com, jetbrains.com/careers, blog.jetbrains.com.
