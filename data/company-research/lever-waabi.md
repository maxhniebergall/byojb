# Waabi — research
Provider/key: lever:waabi | company_type: product

What they do: Waabi develops Physical AI technology for autonomous trucks and robotaxis. Their flagship product is a unified AI platform ("shared brain") that powers multiple vehicle types. They use a simulation-first safety approach with mixed-reality testing and have achieved highway and surface-street driving capabilities. Their model is direct-to-customer trucking (unlike competitor hub-and-spoke models). Waabi was founded in 2021 by Raquel Urtasun (former Chief Scientist at Uber ATG and Professor at University of Toronto).

How they describe themselves: "Pioneering the future of Physical AI." Eight core values: Scientific (objectivity and integrity), Curious (ask difficult questions), Diverse (respectful discourse), Resilient (creative problem-solving), Accountable (follow through), Safe ("non-negotiable"), Efficient (reliability over speed), Data Driven (evidence to validate decisions). They describe "5 North American offices and remote flexibility, you can learn and thrive wherever you work best."

Size / stage / funding: $1 billion in new funding as of January 2026. CNBC Disruptor 50 list (June 2025). Launched driverless commercial trucks in 2025. Major partnerships with Volvo, Uber Freight, NVIDIA. HQ Toronto. ~hundreds of employees (exact count not publicly confirmed). Well-funded growth stage.

Locations / HQ: Toronto, Canada (HQ). San Francisco CA, Dallas TX, Pittsburgh PA. Five North American offices total. Strong University of Toronto ties (founded by UofT professor).

Remote policy: "5 North American offices and remote flexibility, you can learn and thrive wherever you work best." Some roles listed as remote-eligible (Cloud Infrastructure Engineer found as USA+Canada remote). The "On-premise Infrastructure Engineer" role is hybrid Dallas TX. Job postings on Lever include both hybrid (office-required) and remote variants. Remote flexibility appears real but role-dependent.

Remote-Canada eligibility: Likely yes for select roles — HQ is in Toronto (Canada), and at least one Cloud Infrastructure Engineer posting explicitly listed USA and Canada remote eligibility. However, many roles (especially hardware/sensor/on-site) require physical presence. Software and data platform roles may be remote-Canada eligible; "on-premise" infra role is Dallas hybrid only. Verify per posting.

Engineering & tech: Autonomous vehicle stack — simulation engines, neural networks, sensor fusion, mapping, localization, SLAM, data pipelines and ML datasets, cloud and on-premise infra, observability/SRE. The listed infrastructure role (on-premise) involves physical hardware and networks as well as cloud. ML Datasets & Data Pipelines role involves cloud storage and data management infrastructure. GPU-heavy training workloads. Stack likely includes Python, C++, Go, Kubernetes, AWS/GCP.

Notable / other: Raquel Urtasun is a leading AI researcher and was a major figure at Uber ATG (acquired). Waabi is one of the most credible pure-play autonomous trucking companies (not just a software layer). Direct-to-customer model differentiates from Waymo/TuSimple/Aurora approaches. Achieved major milestone in Nov 2025 with surface-street driving. High-profile investors. "On-premise infrastructure" role is notably hands-on physical hardware focused.
